When did Bella Thorne join OnlyFans?

She starred on Disney Channel's 'Shake It Up' as Cece Jones for three seasons, from 2010 to 2013.

Bella, who has three siblings - Dani, Kaili, and Remi - is the daughter of Tamara and Reinaldo Thorne.

The site crashed for a short time when Bella debuted her page.

What is OnlyFans?

The adult social network OnlyFans allows a number of content creators, including sex workers, to interact with their fans and make money while doing so.

Other areas of content include fitness, beauty, and cooking.

OnlyFans is a subscription content service based in London - with an app that reportedly has 60million users.

Sex workers charge monthly fees for access to images, video and more.

Its roster includes 700,000 performers, according to OnlyFans.

"Whether you’re uploading tutorials, tips, behind-the-scenes footage or just endless selfies, a lot of your followers would be willing to pay for them," states OnlyFans.

For every subscription that's sold to a viewer, performers get 80 percent of the cash while OnlyFans gets the rest.

The company was founded by Timothy Stokely in 2016.

How much will Bella's OnlyFans subscription cost?

The cost to subscribe to Bella's OnlyFans is $20 per month.

Subscription bundles are also offered.

Fans can pay $60 for a three-month subscription or $102 for six months - which is advertised as 15 percent off.

When was there an OnlyFans leak?

Back in April, thousands of raunchy pictures and videos leaked online.

The OnlyFans files were dumped into huge databases and shared across the internet by cyber crooks.

However, OnlyFans claimed that it had not been hacked.

Instead, the leak appeared to be the result of a coordinated online effort by users to steal and share as much of the site's content as possible.
