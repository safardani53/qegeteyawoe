GMA fans fear for Ginger Zee after she shares 'horrifying' new video from her new location away from

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

GINGER Zee has struck concern with Good Morning America fans on Tuesday morning.

She shared a video of bugs to her Instagram, as she filmed a segment away from the studio.

Ginger has been away from the studio for a few days to report on Tulare Lake, which used to be the "largest body of water in the American West."

The meteorologist was finally ready to report on the Californian lake on Tuesday when she stumbled upon a horrifying sight.

"Good morning! We have thousands of friends at our live shot this morning. #bugs #tularelake," she captioned her video.

Ginger walked down a dark road toward the cameras and the crew set-up.

Reflecting back on the lights were dozens and dozens of flying bugs.

She did not seem bothered by them though as she walked right up to them before the video ended.

The crew also seemed unphased as they set up their equipment.

Ginger's water bottle was set on a podium in front of the lights right where the bugs were.

The bugs could be seen on the broadcast as well, as Ginger reported on the lake.

'HORRIFYING'

Some of her followers were grossed out after she shared the video of the bugs to Instagram.

They took to the comments to express their disgust.

"That is horrifying and I would quit then and there," said one fan.

"We don’t do bugs! That’s a hard NO!" wrote a GMA watcher.

Ginger's husband, Ben Aaron, said: "Eff that!"

"YIKES!!! That’s the day GMA would fire me, I don’t do bugs," another commented.

"Simply horrifying," said another.

Many of her followers simply said "nope," "eww," and "gross."

WHERE IN ZEE WORLD?

Ginger has been absent from the show's studio so far this week due to her coverage in California.

However, she was away from GMA last week as well for another reason.

Fan-favorite meteorologist Sam Champion took over her role in the meantime.

Ginger was not on GMA on Wednesday or Thursday.

Instead, Sam reported the weather for the morning show on those days.

He typically fills in for Ginger when she is out and is a popular face on GMA.

Fans were wondering where the Chief Meteorologist was, and she explained in an Instagram post.

"There was no better place than @ametsoc #AMS50BC to #showyourstripes — I had a chance to be the keynote speaker with the bright lights in our industry— so grateful to have this time to connect," she wrote.

With the conference taking place in Arizona, and the morning show in New York City, it was impossible for Ginger to get back in time.
