Andi Dorfman and Josh Murray: Moving to Hollywood?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 12, 2024

Well, if this doesn’t signal that Andi Dorfman and Josh Murray are moving to L.A. (most likely to compete on Dancing With the Stars), we don’t know what will:

They just hired a marketing and PR firm to represent them!

Andi and Josh are now represented by MELT, as she confirmed via Instagram with the photo above. Looks like someone’s looking to make some bucks!

Given that Andi quit her job at the Atlanta District Attorney’s office, and then the rumors that one or both of them will be on DWTS, it’s not surprising.

Plenty of Bachelor and Bachelorette cast members go on to have careers in the industry, which is more than understandable, even largely expected.

They just didn’t seem like the types. To us anyway.

That said, it doesn’t make the connection of Andi Dorfman and Josh Murray any less legitimate. Reality TV has given them fame. It is what it is.

 

Some people (Nikki Ferrell and Juan Pablo Galavis) beat a hasty retreat and go back to their lives ASAP, with no love lost when it comes to ABC.

Others look to cash in and have a good time.

It’s a personal choice, and one we wouldn’t judge either way. Nor would we suggest that Andi and Josh are just using their relationship for fame.

Because of how they met, we’re sure someone will say that though, sure as The Bachelor spoilers will leak months before the new season even airs.

By next week, we’ll know if Andi and Josh are going Dancing With the Stars. Until then, take a look back at the couple’s romance in the gallery below!
