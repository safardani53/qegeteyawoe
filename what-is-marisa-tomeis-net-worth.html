What is Marisa Tomei's net worth?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 14, 2024

SPIDER-MAN actress, Marisa Tomei, has won multiple awards for her work on screen.

She has garnered quite a name for herself in the movie industry.

What is Marisa Tomei's net worth?

Marisa Tomei's net worth is estimated to be around $25million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Tomei, 57, was born in Brooklyn, New York to parents of Italian descent.

She rose to stardom after appearing on the soap opera, As the World Turns, in 1984.

What movies has Marisa Tomei starred in?

Tomei has also been featured in movies like: What Women Want in 2000, Anger Management in 2003, Wild Hogs in 2007, Parental Guidance in 2012 and more.

She is well known as the face of May Parker in the Marvel Cinematic Universe and was even cast along side comedian, Pete Davidson, in the 2020 film, The King of Staten Island.

Tomei has won one Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, two Golden Globe Awards, and three Screen Actors Guild Awards.

Is Marisa Tomei married?

Tomei has never been married, nor does she have any children.

According to Ranker.com, her longest relationship was to actor, Logan Marshall-Green.

The two were reportedly dating between 2008 and 2012.

Tomei has also previously been linked to high-profile actors like: Robert Downey Jr. in 1994, Frank Pugliese from 2001 to 2004, Nicholas Carpenter from 2006 to 2008, and Josh Radnor in 2014.
