Katie Price 'wants to make a sex tape with new man Kris Boyson to prove she's still a sex symbol and

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

KATIE Price is said to be planning a sex tape with new boyfriend Kris Boyson to make money quickly and prove she's still a sex symbol.

The 40-year-old star is reportedly on the verge of going bankrupt and it's believed she thinks an X-rated video will boost her income and glamour model status.

A source told Closer magazine: "Katie is desperate to still be seen as a sex symbol and wants to make money quickly. She thinks a sex tape is the best way to get back at her exes."

Mum-of-five Katie made a sex tape with boyfriend Dane Bowers in the 90s which was leaked and shared online and admitted back then that she was "into it".

But the source thinks her reasons this time run deeper than needing money and a confidence boost after her recent split from husband Kieran Hayler.

They added to the magazine: "Katie is essentially dealing with this break-up by numbing her inner turmoil with short-term highs - the going out, the highs of a new relationship. even the highs of flaunting her body in public.

"It appears to be bravado, but I suspect her self-worth is very low at this point.

"For a woman who has made a career out of her looks, getting older will be devastating for her, too."

Last week, Katie hit out in a post on Instagram that was believed to be directed at her exes including Peter Andre, Kieran Hayler and Alex Reid.

She said:"Here's how I'm going to beat you. I'm going to outwork you. That's it. That's all there is to it."

It comes after it was revealed she's selling off her Sussex mansion to pay off mounting debt.

The house in Horsham, West Sussex, costs Katie around £10,000 a month to maintain and it's been reported she's now trying to offload it to save some cash.
