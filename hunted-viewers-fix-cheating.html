Hunted viewers brand show a 'fix' and accuse hunters of 'cheating' as they catch ALL fugitives for t

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 2, 2024

VIEWERS of Channel 4 show Hunted flocked to social media to blast the episode and accuse the hunters of “cheating” as they managed to catch all the fugitives for the first time ever in the explosive final episode.

Fans tweeted their disbelief when the extraction point ended up being on a car park roof and claimed it would have been “impossible” for the contestants to win.

One fan fumed: “Invested 6 hours of my life on this year’s #Hunted for a security guard to give away the extraction location and make it impossible for anyone to win.”

While another demanded: “To theHunted team, you have to pay those top lads, Nate & Frank. They made, you owe them - impossible task, cough up guys - they won #Hunted”

One user questioned the hunters’ methods, tweeting: “Isnt it cheating if you literally just sit at the extraction point... like what are the contestants supposed to do? Have a massive game of tag on the roof whilst the helicopter lands? #hunted”

Ish was the first to get caught after his bike got a puncture and he had to stop under a bridge.

The hunters were keen to get their hands on his notebook and used it to recover information.

After taking him in for questioning, they offered him £4,000 to give up the extraction point but Ish stood firm and told them he wouldn’t betray his fellow fugitives.

On making it to Birmingham the hunters quickly caught up with Harry, and just narrowly captured Nate and Frank as they made their way to the top of the car park.

For viewers it seemed too easy for the hunters to suddenly swoop in and capture the remaining contestants.

One fan wrote: “Genuinely gutted for Frank. Impossible extraction point and he did so well to make it to the roof.”

While another lamented: “Frank Harry and Nate should have won! They still got to the end!”

One tweeted: “#Hunted is so fake. Absolutely tragic.”

Another joked: “Hunters: Ok we'll go to Birmingham
Producers: Ok maybe try this road
Hunters: Oh, look there's Nate walking down the road.”

As the hunters closed in, fans of the show couldn’t hide their outrage as it seemed the end goal for the fugitives was near impossible.

One wrote: #hunted is lying cheating bulls***. Clearly fixed. Won’t be watching again. #channel4 Don’t treat your viewers like idiots”

And another added: “Those two boys who got to the car park should have won. The chosen extraction point was way too easy for the hunters to take advantage of. Try not cheating so much next year, hunters!”
