Fake Pregnancy? Fans Freak Out Over Baby Bump Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 14, 2024

That noticeable change in the size of Kim’s baby bump from day to night seriously made a whole lot of folks do a double take. The reality star has been through her fair share of accusations that she’s faking her pregnancy, but the look of Kim’s bump in the same tight dress in the afternoon and later in the evening didn’t do a whole lot to dispel the rumors. One fan called out Kim’s recent trip to Paris, saying, “Fly’s to London (moon bump.com) for fittings & to Paris to check on the surrogate. Last time, she posed in a bikini and totally nude. Hasn’t this time. Finally discovered dignity? Naw… go on, Kim. Prove us all wrong. Dare you.”

Kim has addressed rumors of having a fake pregnancy before, including this tweet she sent out on July 13. “I’m at the stage now where I wake up with a flat belly but then as I eat and by the end of the day it pops out! This means it’s about to pop,” the 34-year-old wrote. Fans aren’t the only one who have wondered if Kim is actually pregnant or not. HollywoodLife.com EXCLUSIVELY learned how Kim feels about the fake pregnancy controversy.

Kim Slams Rumors Of Her Using A Surrogate

At the beginning of June, the reality star caught wind of Wendy Williams, 51, claiming Kim and Kanye West, 38, were using a surrogate. The talkshow host said the couple, “could possibly be using a surrogate from Thailand who’s allegedly is living in Kanye’s Manhattan Beach home.” Wendy’s comment left Kim infuriated. “Can’t wait til my website is active! I’m gonna do live video streaming so every time someone talks shit I can go blast the f**k outta them,” Kim tweeted. Well, that may have stopped the rumor mill for a couple of weeks but now these new pictures have sent tongues wagging again. Read some of her fans reactions to the bump size below.

Despite what fans may think, HollywoodLife.com‘s photo director David Allen believes lighting and other factors can make a big difference. “People look significantly different being shot in daylight as opposed to being shot indoors,” David said. “The candid shot of Kim during the day could’ve been shot with flash and it also could’ve been taken at a higher angle. The selfie Kim took is more eye level so you’re seeing more underneath her bump. There’s a deeper shadow than when she was in the daylight and that plays a huge part in making it look bigger.” Click to hear more details about the Kim baby bump debate.
