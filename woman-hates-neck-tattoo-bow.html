I wanted a cute bow neck tattoo but it turned out terribly - I hate that its on my skin &amp; cant

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, May 23, 2024

SHE wanted a cute bow tattoo that went round her entire neck.

But Maya was left fuming with the results of the inking, explaining to her followers on TikTok that she "hates" how it turned out.

She began her video series by telling fans to "come with me" as she got the inking.

But she then returned to the clip later on as she admitted she'd been left in floods of tears by the finished design.

"It turned out so bad - I literally just stopped crying. I’m probably not going to show it right now because I feel hella sensitive about it, but when I get it fixed I’ll show it," she said.

"But yeah, I can’t believe I got a neck tattoo that I hate, FML."

She then did a story time about the tattooing experience, as she revealed she'd gone to a tattooist in Reno, California, without knowing that they weren't even licensed for large tattoos.

Maya also ignored warning signs such as the tattooist not cleaning the skin beforehand, not using green soap throughout the inking process and not suggesting that she uses Aquaphor as part of her aftercare.

"Just overall really sketch," she sighed.

"I left a bad review and I didn’t tip - first time I’ve ever done that. I’ve got over 20 tattoos and I have never not tipped.

"I will make a video later today showing the tattoo.

"I just haven’t because I f**king hate looking at it, and it just bums me out - I don’t really want to show it because I f**king hate that it’s on my skin."

She stuck to her word and returned with another video in which she showed off the tattoo.

Instead of the thick red line with a bow that she'd wanted, she's been left with a very thin, shaky line going the whole way round her neck.

"This is the tattoo - it’s just not what I wanted," she explained.  

"I will be thickening up the lines and hopefully it will be something I love."

Maya later revealed she's made an appointment with a friend's tattooist boyfriend to have the design fixed later this month.
