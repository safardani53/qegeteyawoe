Cheryl Hines: Whats In My Bag?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 30, 2024

Cheryl Hines’ leather Rag & Bone tote is ready for international adventure. “I have my passport in case someone wants to whisk me off to Paris,” the actress, 51, jokes. “I could live out of this bag, easily.” The star of Fox’s Son of Zorn (Sundays, 8:30 p.m. ET), wed to activist Robert F. Kennedy Jr., shares her must-haves along for the ride exclusively with Us Weekly.

The Whole Tooth

“You’ll find a lot of dental care in my purse. I love having fresh breath! I have those loose toothbrushes that can’t be clean – but what if I’m desperate and they’re my only option?”

Discount Denied

“CVS gives you these long receipts [with coupons]. I’ll carry one around for at least three months, thinking, ‘If I use it, I’ll get $12 off!’ And when I try to, it’ll be expired.”

About Face

“I’m going to look at this brochure for the Kybella double-chin treatment when I have a moment. I don’t feel like I have a chin problem, but someone gave me it so I feel like I should look at it.”

Bandage Dress

“My daughter [Catherine, 12, with ex Paul Young] had something on her leg, so she needed these big Band-Aids. When you’re a mom, you just have them.”

Game Time

“I have Trivia Crack on my iPhone 6 Plus. We play on set for hours!”
