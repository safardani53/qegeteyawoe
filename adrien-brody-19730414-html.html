Adrien Brody (Movie Actor) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 30, 2024

Adrien Nicholas Brody is an American actor and producer. He received widespread recognition and acclaim after starring as Władysław Szpilman in Roman Polanski's The Pianist, for which he won the Academy Award for Best Actor at age 29, becoming the youngest actor to win in that category. Brody is the second male American actor after Christopher Lambert to receive the César Award for Best Actor. Other successful films that Brody has starred in are The Thin Red Line, The Village, King Kong, Predators and Midnight in Paris. He is a frequent collaborator of Wes Anderson's, having starred in four of Anderson's films, The Darjeeling Limited, Fantastic Mr. Fox, The Grand Budapest Hotel, and The French Dispatch. In 2017, he portrayed Luca Changretta in the fourth season of the BBC series Peaky Blinders. He will star in the upcoming Marilyn Monroe biopic Blonde. In 2022, he also starred as Pat Riley in the first season of the HBO sports drama series Winning Time: The Rise of the Lakers Dynasty.
