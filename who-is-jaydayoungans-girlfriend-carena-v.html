Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Who is CaRena V?

Jaydayoungan's girlfriend, CaRena Vonchae, is a 19-year-old influencer and model.

She has over 285,000 followers on Instagram where she regularly posts photos of her daily life and also recently launched a YouTube channel.

She also has 150,000 followers on TikTok and often posts lip-sync and challenge content.

Aside from social media, she is an aspiring entrepreneur and hairstylist, with a hair salon in Richmond, Atlanta, called CaRena Does Hair.
