Blac Chyna Has Two Children From Two Different Fathers

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 16, 2024

Blac Chyna has two children from two different fathers. She gave birth to her son, King Cairo Stevenson, with Tyga in 2012, and after their divorce, she began dating Rob Kardashian, with whom she has a daughter, Dream Renee Kardashian.

Blac Chyna’s Net Worth

Despite the fact that Blac has been in the news recently for personal reasons, her rise to fame is admirable. She has a net worth of $10 million. Blac rose to prominence in 2010 as Nicki Minaj’s stunt double in the Monster music video. When Drake mentioned her in the song Miss Me, she drew even more attention to herself.

She went on to launch her own makeup line, Lashed by Blac Chyna, as well as a beauty salon in Los Angeles called Encino.

Blac’s OnlyFans venture is said to have contributed significantly to her fortune. In 2020, All Hip Hop reported that she was one of the platform’s highest-paid content creators. She’s also appeared on reality shows like Ron & Chyna and The Real Blac Chyna.

What Dis The Model Have To Say

Blac suggested in a series of tweets on Wednesday that she was receiving “no support” from her former partners. She stated in the first tweet that she had given up her vehicles because she was not being supported as a single mother.

“I had to give up three of my cars yesterday… my reasons… I’m a MAMA with morals, beliefs, being a single mother, and no support.”

In the following tweet, she reiterated the claim, writing, “Single no support child support.” Just as fans began to respond to her tweets, Blac wrote in her final tweet, “Practicing gratitude.”

Tyga And Rob Kardashian Return

Despite the fact that the model did not name names, her ex-partners were quick to respond with their side of the story.

Tyga wrote under a screenshot of Blac’s tweet shared by The Shade Room, “I pay 40k a year for my son’s school & he lives with me Mon-Sat.” “I’m not sure why I’d pay child support.”

Rob’s response from his verified Instagram account is also among the comments.

“Pay 37k a year for my daughter’s school,” Kardashian responds. I am in charge of all medical expenses. All of her extracurricular activities are paid for by me. “From Tuesday to Saturday, I have my daughter. “I’m not sure why I’d pay child support.”
