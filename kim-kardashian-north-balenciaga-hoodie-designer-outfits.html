Kim Kardashian dresses daughter North, 9, in $1K custom-made Balenciaga hoodie after mom was slammed

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, March 7, 2024

KIM Kardashian has dressed her daughter North, 9, in a $1K custom-made Balenciaga hoodie.

It comes after the famous mom was accused by fans of spoiling her daughter.

North was spotted wearing the pricey item as she accompanied her mom Kim, 41, and her grandma Kris Jenner, 66, to the Musée des Arts Décoratifs and Le Louvre in Paris.

Personalized by Spanish fashion designer Balenciaga, the black hoodie featured North’s name in big white letters.

Balenciaga is a firm favorite of Kim’s, and she even walked the runway for the brand earlier this week.

North matched her custom hoodie with oversized sunglasses and loose-fitting denim jeans.

Hulu star Kim wrapped up in a leather coat, long black skirt, and black high heels as she held her daughter’s hand.

She broke up the monochrome look by clutching a silver handbag.

Kim later shared a close-up of her daughter's hoodie on her Instagram Stories.

The family made their way to the French capital for Paris Fashion Week.

NO KIDDING

The event has given both Kim and her eldest daughter plenty of opportunities to steal the spotlight with their choice of outfits.

Earlier this week, Kim dressed North in her dad Kanye West’s famed varsity jacket, which is worth $10,000.

The pre-teen styled it with black wide-leg pants and chunky studded black boots, similar again to the rapper dad’s signature style.

Meanwhile, Kim opted for a green camo-print cropped T-Shirt and neon leggings as they strutted the streets of Paris together.

For another look, Kim and North accessorized their already coordinated pinstriped outfits by adding matching nose rings.

Although the nose rings were fake, some fans took issue with North wearing the accessory and accused Kim of allowing her daughter to act too grown up.

‘I WANT THE MONEY’

Their trip to Paris comes after Kim was accused of "spoiling her kids" because of comments North made to her mom.

During a recent appearance in New York, the beauty mogul revealed to fans how North told her that she is going to "takeover Yeezy and SKIMS".

Kim said: "I asked her the other day...you know you have to wake up early and you have to do this and that… if you want to run SKIMS and Yeezy.

“Then she looked at me...and said 'Run it? I just said I want the money from it.'"

North is one of four children that Kim shares with her ex-husband Kanye, including Saint, six; Chicago, four; and Psalm, three.
