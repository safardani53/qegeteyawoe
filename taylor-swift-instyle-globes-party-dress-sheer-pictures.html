Taylor Swift Dons Sexiest Dress Ever For InStyle Golden Globes Party Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Taylor Swift’s ‘InStyle’ Golden Globes Party Dress — Shocks In Sheer:

The blonde beauty selected an ensemble from British designer Julien Macdonald‘s Spring/Summer 2014 collection and she smouldered as she put her figure on full display in the little knit number. The long-sleeved ensemble featured peek-a-boo detailing, thanks to the knit material of the dress.

Jennifer Lawrence Photobombs Taylor Swift – Golden Globes

She polished off the sexy get-up by pairing it with Jimmy Choo sandals and Lorraine Schwartz jewels — I loved the way her statement earrings complimented the intricate detailing of the dress. While her curls were tucked under into a modified bob at the show, she let down her hair and rocked wavy tresses, which were swept to one side, and her signature red lip for the party.

I love the way the star proved that her style can be so versatile, selecting a dress that was totally different from her red carpet ensemble — and she rocked both red carpets! While her Globes dress was breathtaking, her little knit number was way more appropriate for the afterparty.

I also think it’s important to point out that this dress definitely isn’t easy to pull off — and Taylor does so with such elegance and ease. She makes the sexy look seem so effortless. What do you think? Do you love her look? Is this the sexiest get-up she has ever worn?
