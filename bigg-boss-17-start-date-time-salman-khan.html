What time and date does Salman Khan's Bigg Boss 17 start?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 4, 2024

AFTER 16 seasons Bigg Boss is back with a bang for the new episodes set to start later this month.

With almost nothing confirmed yet for the iconic Indian TV show this years season has got fans desperate to know what to expect.

When will Salman Khan's Bigg Boss 17 start and at what time?

The show is set to premiere on October 15, and has fans super excited after promises that this season will be something truly unique.

It comes just two months after Bigg Boss OTT 2 finished meaning they haven’t had to wait long for their favourite show to return.

Currently the contestants and the house have been kept under wraps by producers as they look to impress viewers when the show starts later this week.

Rumours of who’s going into the house have been picking up recently as TV star Isha Malviya hit back at rumours suggesting her potential entry in Bigg Boss 17.

The actress strongly denied all claims and called them “baseless rumours” while chatting with paparazzi on the streets of Mumbai. 

So far seven contestants have been confirmed to enter the Bigg Boss house.

Influencer Celesti Bairagey, actor Kanwar Dhillon and his girlfriend Alice Kaushik and YouTuber Harsh Beniwal are all confirmed housemates.

Ankita Lokhande, one of the highest paid Indian TV actresses is also set to enter alongside her husband Vick Jain.

Neil Bhatt has been heavily rumoured and his wife Aishwarya Sharma could join him in what is shaping up to be a fantastic season.

How can I watch Bigg Boss 17?

A promotional video shared to social media last month revealed the series will air on Colors TV.

Underneath the video shared by the TV company, reads the caption: "This time Bigg Boss will show a different color, seeing which you will be shocked.

"Watch Bigg Boss 17 soon only on Colors."

Fans of the show flooded the comment section, saying they could not wait for the show to start.

One person wrote: "Wow I am sooooooooooo excited, waiting."

Another said: "Yesss finally."

Someone else shared they were a fan of host Salman Khan's style: "Love @beingsalmankhan new Look!"

The upcoming season will be Khan's 14th as host, with filming already in full swing.

It will also be shown on the Jio App that allows fans to stream the show whenever and wherever they want after it first airs. 

What is the theme of Bigg Boss 17?

This season’s theme is set to be around detectives and solving mysteries as the host dropped a major hint about the unique concept.

The actor, dressed like a detective, said insiders in the house will be "eyes, ears, and brains of Bigg Boss".

Contestants will forge alliances and will be given "unprecedented" luxuries and duties.

Although nothing has been officially confirmed yet a mystery style theme is the best bet.
