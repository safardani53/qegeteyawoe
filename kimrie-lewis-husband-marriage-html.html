Kimrie Lewis' Husband's Identify Is a Mystery Facts about Their Marriage

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, February 3, 2024

Kimrie Lewis is best known for her work in Single Parents and Kenan. While in the former, we see her as a single mom raising her son, in the latter we see her as Kenan Thompson’s close friend. Her character, Mika and Kenan have great on-screen chemistry and it is later revealed that Kenan has feelings for her.

Lewis has played a plethora of characters in her acting career, embracing the roles of a reporter, army recruiter, nurse, and executive producer to name a few. And she is very passionate about her works, often filling her social media pages about them.

However, unlike her openness about her work, Lewis is very low key about her personal life. She has only talked about her personal life in a few instances. In one of her stand ups at the 3rd annual Burbank Comedy Festival in 2016, the actress revealed a little about her husband.

Who Is Kimrie Lewis Married To?

The identity of Lewis’s partner remains a mystery. In her aforementioned stand up, the actress revealed her husband was 14 years older than her.

She also said that she and her husband weren’t planning on having a child soon, joking that it would be too much trouble. While she is not a mother in actual life, she is very close to Devin Trey Campbell, who plays her son Rory in ABC’s sitcom Single Parents.

The actress wished Campbell a happy 12th birthday, saying she was lucky to have played his TV momma.

Single Parents ran for two seasons before being canceled because of lackluster performance. TV Line reported that the second season of the sitcom averaged 0.6 demo rating and 2.6 million total viewers, down 32 and 24 percent from the first season run.

Lewis played Poppy Banks, an owner of a wine-brary in Single Parents.

Comedian And Writer

Lewis graduated from New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts. Having started her professional acting career in 2004, she has been a frequently seen face in TV series.

But aside from acting, the actress is also a comedian and writer. She has been performing stand-up at clubs and colleges for a long time She is also a regular at Flappers in Burbank.

As for writing, Lewis wrote an episode for the 2019 comedy series The Donors. The series is on Kevin Hart’s Laugh Out Loud Network. marriage
