Sofía Margarita Vergara Vergara is a Colombian-American actress, television producer, host, and model. She was the highest-paid actress on US television for seven consecutive years, from 2013 to 2020. Vergara emerged by co-hosting two TV shows for the Spanish TV channel Univisión in the late 1990s. . Her first notable acting work in English was in the movie Chasing Papi. She later appeared in Four Brothers and in Tyler Perry's comedy Meet the Browns and Madea Goes to Jail, receiving an ALMA Award nomination for the sequel. In 2009 she started playing Gloria Delgado-Pritchett in the ABC comedy series Modern Family, she was nominated for 5 Golden Globe Awards, 4 Primetime Emmy Awards and 7 Screen Actors Guild Awards. Vergara has also played roles in The Smurfs, New Years, The Three Stooges, Machete Kills, Chef and Hot Pursuit.
