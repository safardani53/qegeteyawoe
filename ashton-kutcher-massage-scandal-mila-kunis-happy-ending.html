Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 22, 2024

“I have never seen any women go in there,” a neighboring Sushi 101 restaurant worker told In Touch. The star of Jobs was spotted exiting the Ancient Therapy massage parlor on Dec. 18, according to the report. Ashton allegedly wasn’t wearing his wedding ring when he left the North Hollywood establishment. While Ashton could have just popped in for a deep tissue rubdown, sources tell In Touch that’s not what the parlor is exactly known for.

“It’s for the men — if you know what I mean.” The parlor is full of “dingy…private and small” room, an insider tells In Touch, while another neighboring business employee claims the place is “shady, if you get my meaning. Men are in and out at all hours of the day of night.”

Ashton’s rep told In Touch that “nothing inappropriate happened and nothing inappropriate was offered to him. Any implication to the contrary is a lie.” His rep also noted that Ashton had no idea of the establishment’s reputation, though according to In Touch, Ancient Therapy has 10 entries on RubMaps.com, a site that claims to be the Internet’s No. 1 massage parlor locator. Half of the reviews openly discuss receiving sexual gratification there, with one satisfied customer claiming he got “a good topless tug,” according to the report.

To see if the reviews were accurate, In Touch claims they sent in a male reporter who allegedly received a massage while his female masseuse straddled him. She also allegedly placed her hands near his private parts multiple times and when the reporter asked for a “happy ending,” she supposedly started to “reach for him again.” Hmmm.

Ashton and Mila have been dogged by multiple reports of their marriage falling apart. The two were reportedly fighting so much that they were on the verge of divorce, but a source close to the couple EXCLUSIVELY told HollywoodLife.com that “talk of any major troubles between the two is a big far-fetched.” Hopefully, this latest report of a “happy ending” is just another fairy tale.
