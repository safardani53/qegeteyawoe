EMOTIONAL Samantha Martial claims her life is being made a misery by the new lover of her Manchester United ex.

Anthony Martial dumped gorgeous Samantha – the mother of their daughter – after branding her a “grandma” and is now dating reality star Melanie Da Cruz.

But jilted Samantha has launched a heart-felt plea on Instagram to be left alone – even claiming her address was given out on social media.
