[PICS] Paris Fashion Week 2016 Kendall Jenner, Gigi Hadid &amp; More On Runway Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, July 28, 2024

Paris Fashion Week is a week long event extravaganza of the world’s best fashion designers, coming together in Paris, France to show off their best pieces of the year. Like all of the international Fashion Weeks (New York, Milan etc), the event includes both spring/summer and autumn/winter shows every year. The show’s venue is always changing, but it always takes place at an extravagant and lavish venue in the city. The week consists of fashion displays from the obvious top French designers like Chanel, Dior and Louis Vuitton, but it also includes men’s and haute couture shows. These are held semiannually for the spring/summer and autumn/winter seasons.

In 2016, the runway at Paris Fashion Week was truly “popping off” (as we all like to say these days). From Gigi Hadid crushing the catwalk during the Elie Saab show, to Kaia Gerber and Kendall Jenner slaying shows for other designers, the event was a huge success.
