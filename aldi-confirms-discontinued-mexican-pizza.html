Aldi confirms it has discontinued popular family favorite and there are no plans to bring it

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, March 15, 2024

ALDI has recently confirmed that shoppers will likely never see a fan favorite again.

The 16-inch Mexican Take & Bake from Aldi was a thin-crusted frozen pizza from the brand Mama Cozzi's.

The pizza came with black olives, pepperoni, seasoned beef patty, peppers, and cheese.

A customer took to Twitter and asked the large retailer if she can expect to see it on shelves anytime soon.

“What does a girl gotta do to bring back the Mexican Take n Bake Pizza?!! It’s been forever and it’s delicious,” she wrote on Twitter.

Aldi quickly responded: "Sorry to be the bearer of bad news, but it's been discontinued and there are no plans to bring it back.”

While customers won’t be seeing that one anytime soon, shoppers can still grab the Deli Pizza Take & Bake just with different toppings.

The grocery store offers two sizes: the 12-inch or 16-inch.

In the 12-inch size, shoppers can choose from the Ultimate Meat Deli Pizza, the Cheese Cauliflower Crust, Veggie Cauliflower Crust, and Pepperoni Cauliflower Crust.

In the 16-inch size, shoppers can grab the Pepperoni, Five Cheese, Supreme, Mega Meat, or the Sausage & Pepperoni.

SAYING SAYONARA

Products may get discontinued for multiple reasons but more likely than not it comes down to low sales and product price.

Since shelf space is a massive expense to grocers, if a product can no longer compete with sales, stores have no choice but to pivot.

This means testing out new options or more of the same as they know it works.

Pizza isn't the only item shoppers are forced to say goodbye to, the retailer also discontinued a popular beverage.

Aldi confirmed via Twitter that the Vista Bay Margarita Hard Seltzers are gone from shelves.

It said: "We hate to be the ones to give bad news, but it's been discontinued!"

Another product that got the ax was the popular parmesan-encrusted tilapia fish.

This one seems to have vanished from shelves without any warning from Aldi as shoppers were shocked to see it go.

The company confirmed via Twitter the item is no longer available, promising to let "buyers know right away" that one fan's favorite was being missed.
