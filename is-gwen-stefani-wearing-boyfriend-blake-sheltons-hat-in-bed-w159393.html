Is Gwen Stefani Wearing Boyfriend Blake Shelton's Hat in Bed?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 16, 2024

#goodmorning gx

A post shared by Gwen Stefani (@gwenstefani) on Dec 9, 2015 at 11:05am PST

Top of the morning to you! Gwen Stefani posted a photo of herself wearing a camouflage hat that looked suspiciously similar to one belonging to her boyfriend, Blake Shelton.

“#goodmorning gx,” Stefani captioned the Instagram snap on Wednesday, Dec. 9. In the selfie, which appears to have been taken in bed, the “Used to Love You” songstress, 46, sports thick-rimmed glasses and looks flawless without makeup.

Immediately, fans reacted to the gorgeous singer’s familiar-looking hat. “Aww she’s wearing Blake’s hat,” wrote one follower, while another added: “Of course she don’t wear camo on the reg.” The No Doubt singer is well-known for her edgier style choices, including her fashion line L.A.M.B.

Shelton hasn’t worn this exact hat, but Us Weekly can confirm that the item was purchased at his ranch in Tishomingo, Okla. The emblem on the cap is from Shelton’s 1200-acre ranch, which Stefani visited this past October.

A rep for Stefani broke the news to Us exclusively in early November that she and her fellow Voice coach, then newly single after divorcing Miranda Lambert, were dating — and they’ve been adorable ever since going public.

In an interview with Extra this past week, Shelton admitted that he will miss having Stefani on Season 10 of The Voice. “I don’t want Gwen to not be on the show,” the “Honeybee” hitmaker, 39, complained. “Why can’t we have a fifth coach?”

His girlfriend, who was sitting in on the same interview, was asked who she will miss most from the NBC competition series. “I wonder . . .” she coyly replied with a smile. “I’m already so devastated . . . that I’m not gonna be around these guys. It’s hurting me.”
