Blac Chyna reveals her new name after undergoing major plastic surgery to remove butt injections and

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 20, 2024

BLAC Chyna has revealed her new name in a video after undergoing a plastic surgery make-under.

The former E! star recently took to Instagram to reveal that she went under the knife to remove her butt injections and breast implants.

During the vulnerable video, taken in the moments before the surgery, Chyna, 34, revealed her planned procedures and discussed the motivations behind undergoing her make-under.

The model told her followers: 'You all have the power to heal your life, and you need to know that," and then signed off by using a moniker not many fans are familiar with, with her birth name, Angela White.

In the post, Chyna filmed herself donning a paper gown while at a doctor's appointment.

The Los Angeles native sported no makeup and a red and black headscarf in the video as she detailed the reason for the visit.

She explained: "As ya'll know, I've been changing my life and changing my ways.

"So one of the things that I feel like is gonna take me to the next level is obviously taking some of these a** shots out," the 34-year-old shared, referring to her butt injections.

Chyna then encouraged her 16.5million followers not to undergo the injections due to silicone's potentially harmful effects on the body.

Buttock implants are silicone-filled devices surgically placed deep within the tissues of your buttock, according to Mount Sinai's website.

The mother of two noted that she had not had any "crazy complications" since getting the injections as a teenager but wanted them removed so she could "grow."

In another video, Chyna kept the camera rolling while she got blood work, still in her paper gown but rocking a red beanie.

She continued documenting the journey in the following days as she underwent the operation, which included "reducing" the size of her rear and breasts.

The former reality star explained her decision to include her breasts, saying she's "passed that stage" in her life.

Chyna also revealed that it was her fifth time getting a boob job and was "hopefully the last."

Additionally, the TV personality shared other parts of her makeover, pointing out that she's "retired" her long manicures for a more natural look.

The following clip showed Chyna dressed in a hospital gown and cap while preparing for the procedure.

She introduced fans to her surgeon, who explained more about what was to come.

In the final video, the socialite gushed over her results while lying in bed in "recovery mode" from the surgeries.

Chyna had numerous bandages over her breasts and midsection and said she was "very happy" about how her transformation turned out.

She echoed her story in her caption: "I want you all to be a part of my life-changing journey. I Reduced my breast and Gluteus maximus.

After receiving an outpour of well-wishes from fans in the comments, Chyna wrote back: "Thank you everyone for your kind words for a speedy recovery," with a slew of emojis.

PLASTIC SURGERIES

Just hours after she posted the video detailing her journey of reducing her butt and boob size, Chyna announced her plans to undergo more surgeries as part of her major makeover.

The former reality star again took to her Stories to share the news, writing: "Removing my face fillers next!!!"

This isn't the first time the beauty mogul opened up about her multiple surgeries over the years.

During a 2019 appearance on the Wendy Williams Show, Chyna reflected on her makeovers, admitting she had gone overboard.

“I’ve had lipo before; I’ve had my breasts done four times,” Chyna told the talk show host.

She also admitted to getting surgery to reduce the size of her breasts.

“I was like, ‘This is just too much,'" she confessed.

Chyna decided to get liposuction on her butt after her six-year-old daughter, Dream, whom she shares with her ex-Rob Kardashian, 35, was born.

“I went and got something done. I got lipo because after I had Dream, it was like, out of control. So I had some of it taken out,” she said.

Chyna is also mom to son King Cairo with her ex, rapper Tyga, 33.

FINANCIAL WOES

The TV star previously made headlines after she tried to claw $108million from the Kardashian family when she filed a lawsuit against them in 2017.

In April 2022, the lawsuit went to trial, but defendants Kris Jenner, 67, Khloe Kardashian, 38, Kylie Jenner, 25, and Kim Kardashian, 42, emerged victorious.

The family was cleared of defaming Chyna and interfering with her contract with E! after her and Rob’s Keeping Up with the Kardashians spin-off show was canceled.

As a result of the loss, Chyna was awarded no damages.

Last month, she shared her money troubles in a video from TikTok star Mister Lewis.
