Amber Portwood Introduces NewLive-InBoyfriend: Teen Mom OG Clip

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Surprise! Amber Portwood is in love again, and no, his name doesn’t rhyme with “marry.” The Teen Mom OG star anxiously introduced her new boyfriend Matt Baier to the MTV camera crew in this new sneak peek from the Monday, April 27 episode. 

“He’s a DJ,” Amber explained to her producer. “We’ve been best friends for months. I wanted to do it right this time. I didn’t want to put him on camera on our f–king first date.”

The mother of little Leah then revealed that her new beau, Matt Baier, has already met her ex Gary Shirley and has moved in to her home. She then brings him out for the cameras to see, and they reveal that they met on Twitter.
