Rachel Bonnetta's Instagram Reveals Her Age and Her Partner

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

Canadian reporter Rachel Bonnetta rose to fame through her brilliant hosting and on-location coverage skills. She covers sports events for Fox Sports and hosts FS1’s first-ever linear sports gambling show, Lock It In — which earned her an Emmy award.

She also garnered acclamation through her work as a psychiatrist in WWE Backstage.

As her career in TV, the sports reporter is also quite active on her social media, where she shares glimpses about her work and personal life. 

What Is Rachel Bonnetta’s Age?

The Emmy award-winning host was born on October 8, 1991.

She celebrated her 25th birthday on October 8, 2016, posting a throwback picture of her childhood encouraging her followers to save trees.

Bonnetta grew up in the suburbs of Toronto, Canada, and spent most of her time there. It was only in 2015 when she moved to New York, United States.

As of now, she lives in Los Angeles, California, where she serves Fox Sports.

Her Instagram Reveals Her Boyfriend

When it comes to her marital status, the talented Bonnetta is yet to share the wedding vows. But the wedding bells may not be very far, for she is in a long-term relationship.

Her eponymous Instagram account reveals that she is dating a man named Pete since January 1, 2016. The pair were in a long-distance relationship for the first two years. 

Bonnetta openly flaunts pictures with Pete on her Instagram handle. On February 15, 2020, she celebrated Valentine’s Day with him.

The duo also adopted a cute pooch on March 26, 2020, and named him Lou. From their pup pictures to the Koala challenge, Bonnetta has openly shared about her relationship on Instagram. 

On May 17, 2020, she gave a haircut to Pete and shared the cute moment with her followers.
