Love Island cast reunited in London for girls night out - but finalist is missing amid feud rumours

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 15, 2024

MOLLY Marsh was once again missing as the Love Island girls reunited last night.

Whitney Adebayo, Jess Harding, Catherine Agbaje and Ella Thomas glammed up for a night on the town.

The girl group shared a series of pictures as they headed to Bagatelle Mayfair for a bite to eat – but fans were quick to spot that Molly was missing.

Molly is currently in New York with Zach and her little sister.

Viewers first noticed that Molly was an outsider during her time on the show when the other girls often appeared together and would ask Molly to take their picture.

Molly, who is now dating Zach Noble after meeting on the show, later opened up about the situation.

Doncaster-born Molly said: “I’ve heard some things about me separating myself from the girls at lunch and things like that.

“And yes I did and it was just from a place of needing time on my own to make sure I’m ok.

“If I didn’t do that then I would’ve have been in a good place.”

Her co-stars then appeared to snub her at the Love Island Reunion.

Maya Jama interviewed Ella and her boyfriend Tyrique, and asked if there had been any WhatsApp groups created.

And viewers were quick to notice that there was no mention of Molly Marsh when Ella listed out who was in their WhatsApp group.

Maya said: “You made some great friends in there. Has there been a WhatsApp group? Who is in the clique? And has there been direct messaging?”

Tyrique said he’s in a couple of "the main WhatsApp groups" and Ella replied by saying: “We’ve got a group chat – Whitney, Cat and Jess.

“We had to, we made that one clique when we got out.”
