Teen Mom Chelsea Houska slammed by 'disappointed' fans for 'expensive' $185 diaper bag in new clothi

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, April 25, 2024

TEEN Mom fans have slammed Chelsea Houska for selling a diaper bag for $185, asking "does it walk itself?"

The mom of three has launched a new collection called Chelsea and Cole for Itzy Ritzy, on Amazon, but although fans were impressed with the stylish designs, the price tags were criticized.

"Crazy price point for a diaper bag," complained one follower, as another added: "I find it so disappointing how expensive you have these items to buy."

One fan commented: "I love it but don't love the price," as one annoyed fan simply wrote: "Overpriced."

But one fan of Chelsea's was so unimpressed they commented: "I love love Chelsea my fav teen mom but why are these bags soo expensive?

"Does it walk itself ? I want to support her financially with this purchase but I live among the poo . This isn’t affordable."

The 29-year-old MTV star is expecting her fourth child, and third with husband Cole DeBoer.

The pair are already parents to 1-year-old daughter Layne and 3-year-old son Watson.

Chelsea is also mom to 11-year-old daughter Aubree.

She recently proudly put her growing baby bump on full display as she showed off the latest pieces from her new clothing line, a collaboration with clothing website Laurie Belles.

The red-haired beauty excitedly showed off her favorite outfits from her collaboration, and rocked a black faux leather studded jacket that retails for $80. 

The edgy piece was completed with a $54 black sweatshirt, $34 torn leggings and a $15 cozy cuffed beanie. 

As the soon to be mom-of-four modeled the pieces, she continually grabbed her belly and noted the comfort in each garment she was wearing.

The South Dakota native told her Instagram followers that she typically wears a size small in her clothing but due to her pregnancy, she went up to a medium. 

Chelsea also raved about the “stretchy” puff sleeve denim jacket from her line that sells for $64.
