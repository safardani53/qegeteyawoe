Oleksandr Usyk vs Daniel Dubois undercard: Who is fighting on huge bill in Poland?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

OLEKSANDR USYK takes on Daniel Dubois TONIGHT in a barnstormer of a heavyweight title fight - but there is an action-packed undercard beforehand.

The Ukrainian hasn't been in action since he beat Anthony Joshua a year ago in Saudi Arabia.

And now he's back and facing yet another Brit who will be looking to derail the former undisputed cruiserweight world champion's reign at heavyweight.

Dubois beat Kevin Lerena last year on the Tyson Fury vs Derek Chisora undercard, but didn't look convincing at all.

The 25-year-old will have to be on his A-game here if he's to pull of the upset.

And to go along with the intriguing main event, there's a decent undercard to boot, with Prince Naseem Hamed's son Aadam making his debut.
