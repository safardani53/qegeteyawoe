Kylie Jenner's New 'Natural' Platinum Locks

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

Going from jet-black hair to platinum blonde isn’t as easy as it looks, but if anyone can pull off the hair transformation it’s wig maven Kylie Jenner. The 19-year-old Lip Kit entrepreneur finally debuted a selfie with her lightened locks on Instagram Tuesday after shocking fans with a sneak peek earlier in the day.

The reality star, who is currently in New York City for NYFW, is definitely feeling herself in the new blonde lob as she showed off two photos on IG as well as several videos and moments on Snapchat. “All my natural hair,” she captioned one snap.

And later in the night, the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star hung out with fellow blonde Hailey Baldwin, telling the Snapchat universe: “Blonder than Hailey!” Dressed in a sculptural coat and mini skirt by Zaid Affas, Mur Mur bodysuit and DSquared heels, Jenner attended Kanye West’s second Madison Square Garden concert with her famous sisters Kendall and Kim.

Over the Labor Day weekend, Kylie took the plunge and dyed her hair the blondest she’s ever gone. Though she contemplated going honey or platinum blonde to her social media followers, it appears she chose to go all the way with icy blonde.

We can’t help but wonder if Jenner’s new platinum blonde ‘do was inspired by a TBT blonde post shared by eldest KarJenner family member Kourtney Kardashian. Or is it because Jenner’s hair has already been likened to a very Kim K circa Paris Fashion Week 2015 sheen?
