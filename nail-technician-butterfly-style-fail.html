I'm a nail technician &amp; did a dramatic butterfly style on a client, I think they look great but not

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 2, 2024

FANCY treating yourself to a fresh set of nails but don't know what style to go for?

Well maybe stick to a simple design, that is if you don't want to be hit with comments from confused haters.

One nail technician went all out on a dramatic butterfly design for her client, but despite the impressive results, not everyone was convinced the style was very practical.

The elaborate acrylic design didn't just feature long nails, but two huge butterfly wings, which the nail artist, Rose, sculpted before adding gems for the finishing touch.

After sharing a clip of the nails online the video racked up a whopping 7.4 millions views.

But some viewers were left totally confused by the over the top design.

Read more on hair and beauty

Most TikTok users couldn't understand why anyone would want nails that are so impractical in the first place.

One wrote: "Hmm how will she use her hands?"

"How are you going to clean yourself in the bathroom? Just asking," a second commented.

And a third joked: "How far can you fly?"

Other viewers appreciated the artistry, but couldn't imagine having to live with the extra design.

"It's nice for pictures, then soak it off," one wrote.

Someone else agreed: "Well- ngl it's creative."
