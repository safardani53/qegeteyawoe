How old is Stephen Mulhern and what is his net worth? The US Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, February 17, 2024

STEPHEN Mulhern swapped magic for TV hosting some years ago and now he's one of the most recognisable presenters in Britain.

The presenter may be a well known face on the box - but how well do fans really know him?

Who is Stephen Mulhern?

Stephen Mulhern was born on April 4, 1977 - which makes him 44 years old.

From Stratford in London, Stephen is a famous English presenter, entertainer and magician best known for his shows on ITV.

His interest in magic started when his father began teaching him tricks before bedtime when he was 11.

He later joined The Magic Circle as the youngest member, which led to an appearance on Blue Peter.

His career as a magician eventually resulted in television gigs when he won the talent show The Big Big Talent Show hosted by Jonathan Ross.

The prize was that he would get to perform in the Royal Variety Performance at the Victoria Palace Theatre with the Queen sitting in the audience.

What is Stephen Mulhern's net worth?

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Stephen is reported to have a fortune of roughly $3million, around £2,600,000.

The Mirror revealed that from February 2018 to February 2019, the presenter earned £3,051,682.

He has raked in the cash for presenting Catchphrase, Big Star’s Little Star and In for a Penny.

Is Stephen Mulhern married?

No he isn't and is very private about his personal life.

During an interview with Celebs Now in February, 2018, Stephen admitted to having been single for four years, insisting that he'd been a "single pringle" for quite some time.

“Last time I went on a date was about four years ago. That makes me sound really sad! I need a life. I need to stop working so much.”

Who has Stephen Mulhern dated?

At the moment, Stephen seems to be single.

He has been linked to a number of famous faces who he is believed to have dated.

Strictly Come Dancing star and EastEnders actress Emma Barton met Stephen while working together on a pantomime, Snow White.

The two started seeing one another, but things fell apart for the couple in 2011 when they decided to call it quits.

He did reveal at the time that the romance had come to a "natural end".

What shows has Stephen Mulhern presented?

Stephen co-presents a radio show on Heart with Emma Willis and has also moved into presenting and narrating TV shows.

He's the king of the spin-off shows having presented Dancing on Ice: Defrosted and  ITV2's Britain's Got More Talent .

He's a regular in pantomime and has acted as host on quiz shows including Pick Me!

Stephen presents ITV's show Big Star's Little Star, a show which sees parents and their children compete to win prizes, and Catchphrase.

MORE ON STEPHEN

He also hosts celebrity version of these shows.

The TV favourite has appeared in four series of Saturday Night Takeaway and presents the spin-off show In For A Penny.

He is also the host of ITV game show Rolling In It.
