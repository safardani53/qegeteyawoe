Kylie Jenner's BFF Stassie Karanikolaou reveals her mom has died as she mourns loss in heartbreaking

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024

KARDASHIAN friend Stassie Karanikolaou has unfortunately lost her mother.

Stassie, 26, is a social media influencer who is very close to Kylie Jenner, 25, and the Kardashian family.

The mourning influencer shared a heartbreaking post on Instagram in honor of her mother.

She shared several throwback photos from when she was a young girl.

The first showed her on her mother's lap sitting on a bed with her older sister next to them.

The next two photos were close-ups of Stassie and her mother while she was very young.

Her mother was featured by herself in the fourth photo while smiling at the camera, and she was stunning.

The fifth photo was very 90s and included Stassie's mother wearing a plastic visor and the influencer wearing a denim vest.

Photo number six shows The Kardashian's bestie as a little baby while laying on her mom's chest.

The final pic is a fabulous photo of her mother in her long-sleeve wedding gown and wearing a tiara.

Stassie captioned the post: "on Monday, June 26th heaven gained someone so special..

"It’s hard to imagine not being able to call you or hug you or cry at literally anything and then laugh at the fact that we’re all such crybabies & so so much more..

"we’ll miss you every day. we know you’re at peace now with papou.. we love you to the moon & back forever. rest in paradise mommy."

A FAMOUS GOODBYE

Most of the Hulu reality show family commented on Stassie's post and expressed their grief.

Khloe wrote, "Stass I am so so sorry. I love you so much. I am forever here for you for ANYTHING."

Kylie simply said, "I love you forever."

The matriarch Kris Jenner commented, "Sending you so much love Stass, and praying for you and your family."

Other celebrities commented like Hailey Bieber who said, "Love you Stass."

WHO IS STASSIE?

Although originally famous by association, Stassie has built a name for herself as a top-ranked influencer and businesswoman.

Born on June 9, 1997, Stassie - real name Anastasia - is a social media influencer and longtime best friend of Kylie.

With over 10.9 million followers on Instagram, Stassie is no stranger to the limelight.

The starlet often joins Kylie and the Kardashians for family events and parties and is known to have a wide array of celebrity friends herself.

In March of 2022, Stassie and fellow influencer Zack Bia joined forces in launching Sunny Vodka.
