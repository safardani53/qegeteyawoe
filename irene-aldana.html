Irene Aldana | Career

Irene joined Mixed Martial Arts at Local Lobo Gym in 2012.

After that, her actual MMA journey started in 2013, when she faced Larissa Pacheco for the vacant Jungle Fight Women’s Bantamweight Championship at Jungle Fight in December 2013.

However, she lost the fight through Technical Knockout, accounting for the first loss of her professional career.

Invicta Fighting Championship

On September 9, 2014, Irene made her debut against Peggy Morgan after signing with Invicta Fighting Championship at Invicta FC 8.

But this time, Irene submitted Morgan with a rear-naked choke in the first round. After her win, she was scheduled to face Marion Reneau at Invicta FC 10: Waterson vs. Tiburcio.

The fight was scheduled for December 5, 2014, but due to Aldana’s ill health condition, the fight was called off.

On February 27, 2015, Aldana was scheduled to face Melanie Lacroix at Invicta FC 11: Cyborg vs. Tweet.

But, LaCroix withdrew at the last moment from the bout for an unknown reason. Colleen Schneider replaced the aftermath.

Irene Aldana won the fight against newcomer Colleen Schneider via a first-round rear-naked choke.

Ultimate Fighting Championship

It was the year 2016 on 1 November 6; Irene signed the dream of life. ‘Ultimate Fighting Championship’ (UFC).

She faced Leslie Smith for her first fight under UFC. However, Irene lost the fight to Leslie by unanimous decision.

Despite losing her first bout, Aldana earned her first fight of the Night bonus award in the Ultimate Fighting Championship.

Similarly, On January 15, 2018, Aldana faced Talita Bernardo at UFC Fight Night: Stephens vs. Choi. She won the fight by unanimous decision.
