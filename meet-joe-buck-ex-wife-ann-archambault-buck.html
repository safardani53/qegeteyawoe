Who is Joe Buck's ex-wife Ann Buck?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, March 22, 2024

FOX Sports sportscaster Joe Buck is nationally recognized for the media outlet's play-by-play call of NFL and MLB games.

Buck will be the final celebrity host of Jeopardy! this season.

Who is Joe Buck's ex-wife Ann Buck?

Before his espousal to Michelle Beisner, the sportscaster was married to Ann Archambault for 18 years.

Archambault was born in November 1969 in St. Louis, Missouri.

The 51-year-old is a graduate of Spring Woods High School in Houston.

The St. Louis native shares two daughters with Buck, Trudy, 21, and Natalie.

After her divorce from Buck, Archambault has lived a modest life in Ladue, St. Louis.

She often makes appearances on her daughter’s Instagram account.

When did Joe Buck and Ann Archambault marry?

Archambault and Buck wedded in 1993.

According to a profile in St. Louis Magazine, Buck proposed to his ex-wife the only way he knew best, on a live radio broadcast.

Buck invited Archambault for the KMOX postgame show at Mike Shannon’s Steaks and Seafood.

As soon as they met at the steak house, Buck took her to the side room where Shannon was also present and popped the big question.

In addition, the whole conversation was being broadcasted via live radio.

Archambault said yes, as the whole crowd applauded.

Who are Joe Buck and Ann Archambault married to now?

In April 2014, Buck married former NFL Broncos cheerleader Beisner.

Beisner, 44, is a feature reporter for ESPN, a Monday Night Countdown crew member, and Monday Night Football.

Buck and Beisner have a set of twin boys together, Wyatt and Blake, 3.

Meanwhile, there is no information about whether or not Archambault has remarried since her divorce from the sportscaster.
