Who is George Clarks Amazing Spaces star Will Hardie? The Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 31, 2024

WILL Hardie shot to fame on the much-loved Channel 4 show, George Clarke's Amazing Spaces.

But when he's not busy working on the popular programme, he works as an innovative craftsman.

Who is Will Hardie?

Will Hardie is a designer, craftsman and maker.

The TV star is the founder of Studio Hardie which specialises in unique and high profile architecture and design projects.

He has produced work for the London Olympics, English Heritage, The National Trust and Design Museums.

Will was in charge of design development for the "Hardie Stool" launched as part of Kvadrat's Hallingdal 65 exhibition in Milan.

The talented star designed a medieval style adventure playground at Battle Abbey in Sussex.

He has previously worked for Haberdashery London, Sam Neal Metalworks and the Philippe Malouin Studio.

What TV shows has Will Hardie starred in?

Will is best known for his work Channel 4's George Clarke's Amazing Spaces, which showcases unconventional living spaces.

It has been airing since 2012, and he works alongside architect George Clarke.

Together they travel around the UK and beyond in search of imaginative design projects.

Examples of innovative designs include a horse box that was converted into a mobile juice bar and a double decker bus that was renovated to become a family home.

It also features Will's ambitious projects such as building a house from scratch in a day.

Will has also appeared as a judge on George Clarke's Shed Of The Year competition.

In addition to his work for Studio Hardie, he produces custom built sheds under the Amazing Sheds brand.

Are George Clarke and Will Hardie friends?

George and Will are great friends and as well as working together, they spend a lot of their free time with each other.

The pair often post pictures of themselves hanging out, or working on a project.
