AGT's craziest live TV moments this season with Howie Mandel's diss at Sofia Vergara and Simon Cowel

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 15, 2024

AMERICA'S Got Talent has just started their live shows this season and already there has been some crazy moments.

Whether they were rude comments or judges dissing each other, a lot of them were jaw-dropping.

The moments happened among the contestants and the judges.

Each season, AGT is unpredicatable.

Sometimes acts makes America and the judges cry while others make them cringe.

For the most part, the judges-Howie Mandel, Sofia Vergara, Simon Cowell, and Heidi Klum- seem to get along, but very rarely they will deliver digs to each other.

And sometimes the judges deliver mean comments to the contestants.

Most recently, Simon showed up late to the live show, and came in part way through the episode.

The live shows just started and all of the moments happened on one episode.

Here are some of the craziest moments that happened on AGT so far this season.

FIRED UP

The first wild moment comes as contestant Ray Wold and his mom, Corinne, were slammed by fans for their dangerous act.

Ray and Corinne made it through to the live shows and for those who do not like fire, the act was tough to watch.

Throughout the act, Ray was on fire himself as he whipped a lasso around, which was also engulfed in flames.

In their first performance, he threw flaming arrows at targets and his mom, causing everyone to cringe and look away.

For this act, Ray put chains on his wrists and neck, and had to try to get out of them, as he laid down, face-first in a bathtub that was on fire.

Luckily, he got himself out of the chains and stood up in the flaming bathtub before he got burnt.

Also during this act, Corrine stepped through a flaming hula hoop and when it landed on the ground, her behind was on fire.

She walked gingerly to a bucket of water before sitting down and extinguishing her butt.

Howie gave them a X, while Simon, and the audience seemed to love it.

WATER YOU DOING?

The next craziest moment happened on the same episode.

An act who came back to perform spat in Heidi's face.

The Human Fountains are part of a Las Vegas act that features memorable AGT groups from throughout the years.

During the first live show of the season, those acts got up on stage to promote the show.

When it came time for The Human Fountains to showcase their act, they called Heidi up to assist them.

They are three men who drink water bottles and spit it out to resemble a "human fountain."

The three men each put water in their mouths and spit it into the other's.

Heidi then filled up her mouth and spat into the guy on the end's mouth.

The trail continued down until it came back around to Heidi again.

When the man looked like he was about to spit into her mouth, the judge cringed.

He encouraged the crowd to cheer and the model shouted: "No! I'm not doing that!"

When the man began to spit, Heidi ducked down, causing him to miss her mouth, and body completely.

Heidi wagged her finger as she stepped away from them, but the men seemed to take it in stride.

WOW-IE, HOWIE!

Another shocking moment happened during the live show, but this time it was between two judges.

When Brynn Cummings, a 12-year-old ventriloquist, came on stage to perform, Howie delivered a jab to Sofia instead of giving the act feedback.

Her act was centered around her puppet, which is a pink skunk, and how she wanted to find love.

Brynn came prepared with a binder full of potential animals for her skunk to date.

The 12-year-old asked Heidi to help the skunk pick out a potential suitor.

While Heidi was successful in finding the puppet the perfect match, a love bird, Howie had a different thought.

He was the first to speak after Brynn's act was over.

Howie said: "You're adorable and you blend magic and ventriloquism and you deserve to be here."

The former gameshow host continued: "If I have one word of advice if you're looking for eligible bachelors, you should have talked to Sofia because she's in the market right now."

Sofia recently got divorced from her husband, Joe Manganiello.

Host Terry Crews came out from behind the stage and scolded the judge for his mean comment.

"No! No! We are not doing that," he yelled before he changed the subject.

However, Sofia didn't seem to mind Howie's jab as she threw her hands up and yelled: "Yeah!"

'GOT IT WRONG!'

Simon was scolded by AGT fans when he had harsh words for a contestant, who performed on the live show.

John Wines delivered a rock 'n roll performance on the guitar, along with background dancers.

Howie, Heidi, and Sofia seemed to have enjoyed John's performance, but Simon did not.

He didn't like it so much so that he buzzed John halfway through his performance.

Simon started off by saying that he didn't think John's wife will be very happy when she watches his performance back.

"I just thought the whole thing-. I don't know what it was. I just felt strange," Simon told the 59-year-old.

The crowd booed the judge before moving on to the other judges' comments, which were a lot nicer.

Fans were not happy with Simon's feedback and took to the comments of where the performance was posted on social media to tell him.

"Why was it strange? He is incredible," asked an Instagram user.
