Who are Toby Keith's children?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 9, 2024

SINCE stepping into the spotlight, Toby Keith's family has grown along with his discography.

Although the country music crooner amassed a large song catalog, fans are curious to know more about Toby's family and children.

Who are Toby Keith's children?

On March 24, 1984, Toby Keith and Tricia Lucus tied the knot after three years of dating.

Following their nuptials, Toby adopted Tricia's first daughter Shelley, who was born in 1980.

A year in the wake of their wedding ceremony, the pair welcomed their second child together.

On September 30, 1985, their second daughter Krystal was born.

The couple waited over a decade to welcome their third child together.

Sometime in 1997, Toby and Tricia's only son Stelen was born.

Who is Krystal Keith?

Krystal is the middle child of Toby and Tricia's three children.

Similar to her superstar father, she is also a country music singer and songwriter.

Krystal is signed to Show Dog Nashville, a record company founded by her dad.

Through Show Dog Nashville, she released her first album, Whiskey & Lace, in December 2013.

On October 23, 2010, she married Andrew Sandubrae and held their wedding ceremony in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico.

Who is Stelen Keith?

Out of Toby and Tricia's three children, Stelen is the only boy and the youngest of the bunch.

Regarding his famous father's career, Stelen told OU Daily: "I’ve seen it all up-close and personal for my entire life."

"I’ve seen from agencies to backstage to production to the production of an actual song to actual performance."

From 2017 to 2020, he ran Nostalgic Nights, a concert booking company he co-founded in Oklahoma.

He also owns 405 Burger Bar, a bar and grill-styled restaurant in the aforementioned state.

On November 9, 2021, Stelen married his longtime girlfriend, Haley.

How many grandchildren does Toby Keith have?

Through his two eldest daughters, Toby and Tricia have four grandchildren.

They have two grandchildren from Shelley and two granddaughters from Krystal.

On February 1, 2019, Krystal announced the news of the arrival of her second daughter - Toby's fourth grandchild - with her husband Andrew through social media.

She wrote on Facebook: "When you thought you had loved as big as you ever could. And then in a single moment, the size of that love doubles."

"Welcome to the world Kirby Kaye Sandubrae. Our sweet girl joined us on Tuesday weighing 7lbs and 14oz."
