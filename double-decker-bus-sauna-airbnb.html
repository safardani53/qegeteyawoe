PARTY BUS Ultra luxurious double decker bus comes with a SAUNA, beer cooler and hot tub

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

A DOUBLE decker bus which sits on a parcel of land equipped with a sauna and hot tub is now available to guests.

Described by the Airbnb hosts as the "ultimate entertaining space," the double decker bus sits next to a main house that comes fully equipped with everything you need for a weekend away.

Located in Fermanagh and Omagh in Northern Ireland, the double decker bus sleeps six guests in "bespoke comfort" with a large king bed, and two sets of bunk beds.

There's also a spacious living area, kitchen and bathroom on board.

The main house has three king rooms and a bunk room which houses two doubles and two singles.

There is an open plan kitchen, living and dining room with concertina doors to an outside deck.

If guests feel like kicking back to watch a movie, there's a large projector and screen in the living area along with 65” sky glass TV.

The walls of the racing-themed double decker bus are covered in bright paintings of sports cars, motorbikes, graffiti fonts, and bright colours.

The bus' exterior also features a feature wall showing the back half of a vintage car.

The bus houses a small bathroom equipped with a shower and toilet, while the main house has two full bathrooms.

Meanwhile, a restored shed attached to the main house is equipped with a hot tub, stove, beer cooler and sauna.

If guests would rather take the party outside, they can enjoy a fire pit and a large outdoor seating area.

The double decker bus and accompanying main house costs around £1050 per night to book.
