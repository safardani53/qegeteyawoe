Jessica White &amp; Nick Cannon Dating? Details On Blossoming Relationship Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 2, 2024

Jessica White & Nick Cannon Dating?

Nick and Jessica love to party together but they also enjoy doing day-to-day tasks together as well! They new couple was spotted grocery shopping in NJ during the day on March 14, according to Page Six.

That night, wearing the same clothes, Jessica joined Nick at mur.mur nightclub at the Borgata in Atlantic City, where Nick was DJing a party.

Nick Cannon Reveals How The Kids Are Coping With Divorce Process

The reason they have been trying to keep their relationship on the down low is because Nick’s divorce from estranged wife Mariah Carey is not final yet, but that didn’t stop him from introducing Jessica to his kids, 3-year-old twins Monroe and Moroccan Scott Cannon.

Nick and Mariah filed for divorce in January and are still working out the issues of money and child custody.

Nick Cannon On His Current Relationship Status

Nick is clearly not ready to come out and address his relationship with Jessica just yet.

He told Extra on March 16, “I’m not dating anyone ... I don’t have time ... I’m trying to focus on being the best father I can possibly be, and business, we have so many things, from the book to my philanthropic efforts to all the shows and movies I’m producing.”
