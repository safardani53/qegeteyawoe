Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

TOMMY Fury has been forced to splurge $300 on a FIVE HOUR Uber to the airport as he prepares to head home from America.

The 22-year-old boxer shared his excitement at finally reuniting with girlfriend Molly-Mae Hague upon his return to the UK, but faces an ordeal to get there.

The star had been in Las Vegas supporting his brother Tyson's victorious fight against Deontay Wilder, where he had video-called Molly live from the ring.

Now that the fight is over, Tommy is returning to their Manchester penthouse - but has had to splash Â£230 on a 300-mile taxi ride first.
