THINGS get very steamy when Elizabeth Hurley strips off for a sex scene with a female lover in a new erotic thriller - directed by her own SON Damien.

The model, 58, romps with actress Pear Chiravara in sizzling scenes for raunchy spine-chiller, Strictly Confidential.

Liz - who plays a character called Lily in the movie - is seen getting physical with her co-star in intimate scenes.

It's the brainchild of her son Damian, 21, who has also penned the racy script.

It will surely leave fans feeling flustered - as the women are seen embracing passionately, before Pear plants a kiss on Liz's bare skin.

The film follows a young woman named Mia, who is haunted by the suicide of her best friend Rebecca.

Reluctantly, she accepts an invitation from Rebecca's family to come to their home in the Caribbean, where her college friends gather to remember Rebecca on the anniversary of her death.

Once on the island, Mia is plagued with suspicions that there is more to Rebecca's death than meets the eye.

She gradually learns that both Rebecca's family and each of the guests harbour a deadly secret, all of which are directly related to Rebecca.

It will see Mia drawn into a world of sex, duplicity and betrayal as she desperately tries to uncover the mystery surrounding her friend.
