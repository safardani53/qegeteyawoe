New Swimsuit Pic From Vacation With Tim &amp; Faith Hill Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, October 9, 2024

Gracie McGraw, 23, is kicking off the year in total relax mode: AKA, enjoying the beach in a swimsuit. Faith Hill and Tim McGraw‘s daughter shared new photos from her family’s tropical getaway on Jan. 5, which featured a selfie of the fashionista smiling in a green, floral-print swimsuit with crystal-clear ocean water behind her. Gracie showed off another one of her swimsuit looks in the slideshow, which was a cute bikini set: a zebra stripe bikini top and olive green bottoms.

There has been one downfall to vacationing in such a sunny place, though: tan lines. However, Gracie revealed that she didn’t get them in the usual area. In a video within the slideshow above, Gracie lifted her bangs to reveal that she was left with…a forehead tan line. Anything is possible!

Gracie also sneaked in a behind-the-scenes photo of herself transforming into George Washington (who actually has ties to Gracie’s dad, which was revealed on an NBC episode of Who Do You Think You Are in 2011). The photo was taken pre-vacation, when Gracie and her family had a Hamilton-themed dinner leading up to Christmas. It was one of the many themed dinners the creative family held: another night was Game of Thrones-themed, while the family all dressed up as Harry Potter characters on a different night.

Under the mix of vacation and throwback photos, Gracie wrote, “Just some things I felt were important…… disclaimer me becoming ole georgie wash was from a month ago but still important.”

Gracie has been stunning on her family getaway with her parents and sisters Maggie, 22, and Audrey, 19, as they continue to vacation in an undisclosed tropical location. The family of five made the trip before New Year’s Eve, and Gracie stunned in this bikini selfie before ringing in 2021. While the family hasn’t given away the whereabouts of their trip, Faith and Tim do own a 20-acre private island in the Bahamas.
