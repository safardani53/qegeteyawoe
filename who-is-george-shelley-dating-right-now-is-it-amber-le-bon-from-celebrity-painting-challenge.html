Celebrity Painting Challenge has us well and truly glued to our screens, as the six celebrities battle it out for a coveted place in the gallery.

Among the contestants is ex-Union J star George Shelley and it seems that viewers suspect he might have the hots for fellow contestant Amber Le Bon.

So who is George Shelley dating? Find out everything we know below…

George Shelley

What is George Shelley’s dating history?

During the height of the X Factor in 2012, George was rumoured to be dating fellow contestant Ella Henderson after they were spotted out on several dates.

In 2015 rumours circulated that George was dating his I’m A Celeb camp mate Ferne McCann before being connected to  Capital Breakfast host Lilah Parsons.

In 2017 George Shelley was reported to be dating uni student and model Matthew Holehouse after coming out as bisexual in 2016.

According to Attitude George and Matthew went public on Instagram in April 2017 in a since-deleted post while on holiday in Costa Rica.

However, given that the photo no longer exists on George’s ‘insta it’s safe to say that the romance is no more.

Is George Shelley dating Amber Le Bon?

Although fans may be detecting chemistry between the two, there is no evidence to suggest the George and Amber are dating.

In 2018 George opened up about his sexuality to his father in a BBC Three film Coming Out To My Dad: George Shelley where he identified as gay rather bisexual. 

From this, we can assume that George and Amber are just good friends, rather than anything more.

Amber’s social media seems to show that is single right now, with her previous relationships including Kings of Leon singer Jared Followill and Gavin & Stacey frontman Mathew Horne.

Is he currently in a relationship?

As of April 2019, it seems that George Shelley is single, with no trace of any relationships on his social media.

Due to being on the new series of BBC One’s Celebrity Painting Challenge its likely that he is too busy perfecting his masterpieces to give time to a relationship.

But we will make sure to keep you updated if this changes any time soon!
