PALOMA FAITH has had four hit albums and sold millions of records worldwide – but she wasn’t always so well off.

She has revealed she once shot a topless lesbian sex scene for an art film to earn enough money to pay her rent.

The British singer faced eviction unless she stumped up the cash, but admitted she did find the experience “hot”.

Asked on an Instagram Live if she had ever had a lesbian fling, Paloma said: “I was with a woman once but only from the waist up and it was when I couldn’t make my rent.

“An artist asked me if I would be in an art film, doing stereotypical lesbian stuff with a woman.

“The woman I did it with was really attractive, awe-inspiringly gorgeous. And I felt like, yes, it was hot.

“I got £250 for it, which is what I needed to cover my rent. I was going to get evicted at the time.”

The Only Love Can Hurt Like This singer is now in a long-term heterosexual relationship with French artist Leyman Lahcine, with whom she shares a three-year-old child who she is raising as gender-neutral.

Paloma admitted the on-screen fondle is her only lesbian experience but previously said she believes most women are bisexual.

Speaking in 2014, she said: “I’m a gay man trapped in a woman’s body.

“I also think most women are bisexual.

“I think it’s maybe less so for men because socially it’s more taboo — there’s more pressure on a man.

“So it’s a big deal, a big decision to come out.

“I think with women, it’s every heterosexual man’s biggest fantasy to see two women get together, so it’s a bit more acceptable.”

Her video would surely be worth far more than £250 now.

And if that theory about straight men is correct, I’m sure her boyfriend wouldn’t be against her digging out the film for him to watch.

Megan cash hits the spot

MEGAN THEE STALLION is one of the lucky people to benefit from the lockdown.

The US rapper’s song Savage has shot up the charts after a dance challenge to it was created on social media app TikTok.

Videos of people doing the short routine have been viewed more than 360million times and show no sign of slowing down.

The tune rose from No28 to No22 in yesterday’s chart.

Megan, seen here in a tiny leopard-print top and undies, is also studying healthcare administration and tweeted on Wednesday that she would donate cash to her fans who are working in the US healthcare sector.

Lockdown will give her ample time to hit the books.

    • Hacienda's house party

    FANS of house music are in for a treat today as legendary Manchester club The Hacienda is resurrected for an epic virtual party.

    Starting at noon, the bash will feature DJs Roger Sanchez and David Morales playing live sets from the US.

    Graeme Park, K Klass, Jon DaSilva and Tom Wainwright will perform from a Manchester studio.

    The “stay at home rave” will also have live interviews throughout the day, with Happy Mondays singer Shaun Ryder and the city’s mayor Andy Burnham in the line-up.

    The Hacienda, which closed in 1997, is streaming the 12-hour event for free on its social channels.

    Those who want to chip in for a ticket have been asked to donate to music therapy organisation Nordoff Robbins and The Greater Manchester Mayor’s Charity.

    MOST READ IN BIZARRE

    A raya hope, Freya?

    ONLINE romance is going through the roof in isolation – and celebrity dating app Raya is seeing a lot of the action.

    My sources spotted Brit Award nominee Freya Ridings on the app, which has previously been used by single stars such as Demi Lovato and Sam Smith to look for their perfect partner.

    A pal of Freya’s said: “She has been incredibly busy launching her singing career over the past few years and finds it hard to meet men on tour.

    “Being on lockdown has given her a chance to chat to some different guys on Raya and get to know them.

    “She never gets a minute to sit still normally, so she’s really appreciating the time to flirt.”

    To attract potential suitors, the singer’s profile page includes pictures of her posing in pretty dresses and a video in which she is belting out Florence and the Machine hit Cosmic Love.

    And there is no shortage of male stars looking for love at the moment.

    Newly single Channing Tatum, Niall Horan and actor Tom Felton are all on the market, so Freya can have her pick.
