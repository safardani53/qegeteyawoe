Messi stays at home while Ronaldo heads to beach as sports stars including Mayweather and the Vardys

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 17, 2024

IT’S the season to be jolly - and the world’s biggest sports stars are getting into the festive spirit.

From matching pyjamas, heavily lit trees, awful looking jumpers and sacks full of presents, the best athletes on the planet have been showing their Christmas cheer.

Barcelona legend Lionel Messi led the way for festive loungewear as he posed with his three children and wife Antonella Roccuzzo decked out in Xmas pyjamas.

His La Liga rival Sergio Ramos went for a more dapper and solo look as he grinned with a glass of classy prosecco with three Champions League trophies behind him.

While Manchester United's Paul Pogba posed with wife Maria and their new son. The World Cup winner wrote: "Family time. #NoToRacismWeAllOne."

Former UFC champion Conor McGregor - who returns to the cage next month - posed with his partner Dee Devlin and two kids.

The Vardys shunned matching pyjamas but instead dressed up their three dogs in bow ties.

Boxing legend Floyd Mayweather - who brought his daughter Iyanna, 19, a £140,000 Mercedes-Benz G63 for Christmas - posed on his own next to a humongous tree.

Mayweather, in typical fashion, was counting money just a day after bragging about topping the Forbes rich list for the decade.

His former welterweight rival Amir Khan, showing off his new silver hair, was dressed in ‘daddy deer’ pyjamas next to ‘mummy deer’ Faryal Makhdoom.
