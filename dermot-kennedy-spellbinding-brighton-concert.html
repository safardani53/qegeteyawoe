Dermot Kennedy puts on a spellbinding performance that leaves Brighton crowd mesmerised

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

THERE are not many concerts where you feel something special is happening before your eyes.

But at his sold-out gig at the Brighton Centre last night, Dermot Kennedy commanded the crowd and cast a spell of musical magic like a raspy Merlin. 

When an artist conveys as much emotion through both their creativity and performance, then there's no need to wonder why, after two number-one albums, Dermot had the eclectic audience eating out of his hands.

His raw sensitivity and lyrical poetry felt like a shared soundtrack to your personal experiences just as much as his. 

Even the lesser-known tracks made an impact and resonated just as much as his more commercially successful hits.

Dermot will no doubt continue to unite and wow audiences across the globe with passion, bravery, and tenacity.

Whether his upward trajectory has been noticed by the masses yet or not, each and every person at that concert had their vocal cords stretched and their heartstrings pulled by this formidable former busking boy from Dublin.

For more details and tickets for the Sonder world tour go to: https://www.dermotkennedy.com/tour/
