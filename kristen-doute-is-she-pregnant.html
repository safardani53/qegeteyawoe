Kristen Doute: Is She Pregnant?!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Kristen Doute posted a tweet that had many Vanderpump Rules wondering if she is with child.

On September 15th, Doute showed off a side-by-side photo with friend (and Jax Taylor’s ex-girlfriend) Carmen.  

Both women were sticking out their stomachs as if they were pregnant.

"Mexican food isn’t the same without you," Doute wrote.

"@CarmenDickman I feel like I’ve successfully matched your push-out-pregnant-belly #success."

What’s weird is that Carmen doesn’t look to be pregnant in any of her other Instagrams, and is a blond, not a brunette.

Naturally, neither woman gave any context to the photo.

ARE THEY PREGNANT? IS JAX THE FATHER…OF BOTH UNBORN CHILDREN?

Doute did clear things up in a Snachat video later (with a deer filter).

"OK, you guys.  I’m really tired but I have to set the record straight," she said before panning the camera over her flat, tanned stomach.

"It was a food baby.  Goodnight."

Doute, 33, and Carter have been living together since last fall, and signed a lease on a new place this past winter.

So, is Carter "The One?"

Doute seems to think so.

"Totally, no question," she told E! News back in February.

"It’s hard to explain," she began

"I was talking to my friend Jade [Roper, Bachelor in Paradise] who just got married, and she told me, ‘To each their own.

‘When you know, you know.’

"The best thing about him is that he’s just very honest and communicative," she continued.

"Our communication skills are spot-on. It works. It just does.

"When we met, we just sort of knew on the first sight. My friends love him, his friends love me. It just works."

It looks like Doute and her Rules crew are back together, because they all went to co-stars Tom Schwartz and Katie Maloney’s August 17th wedding in Northern California.

"I’m so happy to have married my best friend and love that I finally get to call Tom my husband," Maloney told Bravo’s The Daily Dish in a statement.

"Celebrating with our closest friends and family has been nothing short of an amazing experience."

Rules is currently filming its fifth season, and yes, Jax Taylor’s girlfriend, Brittany Cartwight is part of SUR’s waitstaff now.
