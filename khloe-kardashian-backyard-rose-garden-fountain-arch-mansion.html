Khloe Kardashian shows off sprawling backyard including rose garden, water fountain and stunning arc

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 30, 2024

KHLOE Kardashian has given her followers a glimpse at her sprawling backyard at her $17million Calabasas mansion.

The Kardashians star took to her Snapchat on Monday to share pictures of the oasis.

In them, Khloe, 39, captured a ton of fish swimming in a pond and an arch over a walkway into the yard.

She also gave an up-close view of her rose garden beside her massive in-ground swimming pool.

After a lengthy building process, the Hulu personality moved into the home in November 2021.

It's directly next door to her mother, Kris Jenner's $20million mansion, in the exclusive gated community.

Khloe initially delayed her move into the home after plans for her baby daddy, Tristan Thompson, 32, to move with her changed.

The Good American founder shares a five-year-old daughter, True, and an 11-month-old son, Tatum, with the NBA star.

CHEATING SCANDAL

They decided to live separately after news broke that Tristan fathered a child with fitness trainer Maralee Nichols, 32, while he was in an exclusive relationship with Khloe.

A source exclusively told The U.S. Sun in January 2022 that Khloe was "delaying moving into the new house."

"But [she] is redoing things and tearing up the old plans she did with Tristan - because she just can't face being in this big huge mansion all by herself."

The insider added: "This was supposed to be the family home - and they were supposed to have baby [number] two by now. Instead, he has Baby number three with a stranger and she's all alone."

Weeks later, Tristan dropped $12.5million on a Hidden Hills mansion in Khloe's neighborhood.

MAJOR MILESTONE

Khloe has insisted that she and Tristan are not back together and are strictly co-parents of their two children.

The TV star recently shared a major milestone moment for their nearly one-year-old son.

She posted a video on social media of the tot crawling around the floor of her home, almost making his way up the stairs.

Tatum wore an adorable brown T-shirt and shorts set with a white bib/bandana and colorful Nike sneakers.

Khloe has primarily kept her son out of the public eye, though he recently appeared on the family's reality series, The Kardashians.

SPECIAL MOMENTS

In May, the mom of two shared a never-before-scene selfie of her, True, and Tatum.

The picture captured the star smirking at the camera and True grinning from ear to ear as they relaxed outside in the sunshine.

Tatum was shown looking off to the side while wearing a firefighter hat, seemingly from his cousin Psalm's fourth birthday party.

Earlier this month, Khloe shared her special bond with her daughter in a sweet video of the duo and her six-year-old niece Dream.

Khloe sang to the camera while True mocked and repeatedly interrupted her mother.
