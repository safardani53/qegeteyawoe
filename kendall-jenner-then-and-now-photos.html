Kendall Jenner Then &amp; Now: Photos Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

From young tween to stunning runway model, see photos of Kendall Jenner through the years in honor of her birthday!

Who knew little Kendall Jenner would grow up to be a catwalk queen? The second youngest of the Kardashian-Jenner bunch was born November 3, 1995, making her just a kid when she first started appearing on TV in the family reality show Keeping Up With The Kardashians. In the years since the show premiered, Kendall went from a precocious horse girl to a full-blown supermodel.

Kendall has walked some of the biggest runways in the world, strutting her stuff at New York Fashion Week and gracing countless magazine covers. And with the launch of her 818 Tequila, she now knows what it takes to be a boss.

Kendall’s effortlessly chic style has made her a fashion icon over the years. Whether she’s in relaxed jeans or a slinky slip dress, the stunner always oozes confidence.

Fans absolutely love how Kendall has grown and matured over the years. To take a look back at the beauty, from her young teen years to the peak of her stardom today, scroll through our gallery here.
