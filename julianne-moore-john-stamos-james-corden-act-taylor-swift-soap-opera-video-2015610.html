Julianne Moore, John Stamos Act Out Taylor Swift Soap Opera: Video

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, March 28, 2024

The Bold and the Bad Blood? Taylor Swift’s lyrics aren’t just catchy, they also have the ability to narrate an entire soap opera. In a new viral sketch from Late Late Show newbie James Corden, two top stars — Oscar winner Julianne Moore and seasoned TV stud John Stamos — acted out their own soapy drama using only lines from Swift’s songs. 

The skit titled “The Bold and the Lyrical,” gave fans the opportunity to see Moore, 54, declare: “You could have been getting down to this sick beat!” while doing a shimmy in her silk lavender robe. 

Host Corden, dressed in a leather jacket and wearing a Stamos-esque wig, added, “‘Cause darling I’m a nightmare dressed like a daydream.” 

And when Moore slapped him, she shouted, “Shake it off!”

Stamos, 52, appeared toward the end of the sketch, noting (with a wink), “I can make the bad guys good for a weekend.”

This isn’t the first sketch poking fun at Swift’s omnipresent influence. On Saturday, Oct. 3, Saturday Night Live mocked the pop star’s massive “squad” in a clip starring Aidy Bryant and Vanessa Bayer. The “Blank Space” singer, 25, has yet to acknowledge either spoof on social media as she continues her 1989 World Tour.
