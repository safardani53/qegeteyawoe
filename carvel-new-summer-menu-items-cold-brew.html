Carvel launches six new sweet items for summer but theres a catch

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 15, 2024

CARVEL has debuted six new treats for the summer – but they might not be available forever.

The ice cream chain recently updated its menu with the new products and it's good news for coffee fans.

Most of the fresh items include a cold brew twist.

Here are some of the new items:

  • Salted caramel cold brew flying saucer – soft served ice cream in between chocolate wafers
  • Salted caramel cold brew sundae dasher – soft-served ice cream with vanilla cookie crumbles, finished with whipped cream and gluten-free vanilla cookie crumbles
  • Salted caramel cold brew shake – blended soft serve ice cream
  • Gluten-free vanilla crumble – a new sugary topping that can be added to your favorites

In addition, fans can order the salted caramel ice cream in either a cup or cone to make it six options in total.

While prices vary by location, most of the new options will range anywhere between $3.99 to $9.69 depending on the type of item ordered and size, according to DoorDash.

Also, a six-pack of salted caramel cold brew flying saucers can be ordered for $16.99.

In each package, there are two sweet treats included.

And keep in mind that availability could vary by location and these items are listed for a limited time only on DoorDash.

MORE MENU CHANGES

But Carvel is far from the only store chain from adding to its menu as the summer approaches.

McDonald's recently brought back a few freeze treats, which are as follows:

  • Frozen Coca-Cola
  • Frozen Fanta Blue Raspberry
  • Frozen Sprite Lymonade

But McDonald's isn't done there, as it will make a major change in honor of the purple puppet Grimace.

On June 12, it will launch a purple shake that's made of vanilla soft serve and berry flavors with whipped cream as the topping.

Also, Dairy Queen is launching a new flavor known as the cake batter cookie dough blizzard.

A spokesperson from Dairy Queen described the new offering as having a: "delicious DQ soft serve, cake batter flavor, [and] confetti cookie dough pieces."

The new blizzard option will be available on June 26 in participating U.S. locations.
