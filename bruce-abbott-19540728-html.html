Bruce Paul Abbott is an American film, theater and television actor. Beginning his career in the theater, Abbott later rose to fame as Dr. Dan Cain in the sci-fi thriller Re-Animator and Bride of Re-Animator. He also played roles in the horror films Bad Dreams and The Prophecy II, as well as the sci-fi film Out of Time. From 1992 to 1993, he played Judge Nicholas Marshall in the Dark Justice television series.
