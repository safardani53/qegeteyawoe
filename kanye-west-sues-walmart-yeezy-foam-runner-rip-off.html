Kanye West sues Walmart for selling Yeezy Foam Runner shoe rip-off

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 12, 2024

KANYE West is suing Walmart for selling low budget rip-offs of his Yeezy Foam Runner.

The 44-year-old rapper claims the retail giant has copied his designs to the detriment of his fashion empire and is now seeking damages.

Court documents state an "unauthorized exact copy" was made of the $75 shoe by Walmart - only to be sold at $25, a fraction of the intended price.

The papers obtained by TMZ also detail how the Foam Runner was introduced in 2019, before making its official debut in 2020, selling out straight away.

With the copycat shoes allegedly being sold in Walmart's online shop, papers state a request to take down the footwear was ignored.

Walmart has since responded to the claim.

A company spokesperson said: "The product referenced in the complaint is not sold by Walmart, but rather by third party Marketplace sellers.

"We take allegations like this seriously and are reviewing the claim.

"We will respond in court as appropriate after we have been served with the complaint."
