Who is Lil Waynes ex-girlfriend, Denise Bidot?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 23, 2024

RAPPER Lil Wayne began dating his ex-girlfriend Denise Bidot in the summer of 2020.

However, the two broke up at the height of 2022 and now people want to know what Denise Bidot is up to.

Who is Denise Bidot?

Denise Bidot is a plus-size model born on June 13, 1986, and of Puerto Rican and Kuwaiti descent.

In 2014, she became the first plus-size model to walk multiple runways during New York Fashion Week.

She has also worked with Target, designer Lilly Pulitzer, and Lane Bryant fashion campaigns. 

Denise starred in the documentary Straight/Curve which was released in the spring of 2017 and was part of HBO Latinoâ€™s hit series Habla Women.

Additionally, Denise is the mother of a teenage daughter named Joselyn but chooses to keep her out of the limelight.

When did Denise Bidot and Lil Wayne break up?

Denise and Lil Wayne broke up in January 2022.

The two went Instagram official in the summer of 2020.

During Lil Wayne's 39th birthday, Denise took to the app to celebrate his big day.

In a since-deleted photo, Denise wrote: "Happy birthday to my favorite human on this or any planet.

"You've been my teacher, friend, and the greatest love of my life."

While it wasn't confirmed why the two broke up, rumors circulated that it was because of their different political views after Lil Wayne was pictured with former president Donald Trump.

What has Denise Bidot said about their breakup?

In January 2022, Denise spoke with Hola! on how she feels after the breakup.

She told the outlet: "Today I feel like a much more complete woman: I learned to love, not only another person as a couple, but the world, life, to value time, my heart has opened."

In an August 2023 interview with the magazine, Denise said she is now ready to date again.

She revealed she was on the dating app Chispa and is excited to date someone of Latin descent.
