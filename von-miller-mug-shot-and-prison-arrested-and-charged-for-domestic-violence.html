Von Miller Mugshot And Prison: Arrested And Charged For Domestic Violence

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, May 26, 2024

Von Miller Mugshot And Prison: The DeSoto Police Department recently shared NFL player Von Miller’s mug shot.

The Buffalo Bills linebacker and DeSoto native turned himself in on Thursday afternoon and is currently out on bond.

He faced accusations of assaulting his pregnant girlfriend at their Dallas apartment this week.

The police issued a warrant for a third-degree felony charge based on sources, officials, and court documents obtained by WFAA on Thursday.

Von Miller is an American football player who played as an outside linebacker in the National Football League (NFL).

He was drafted by the Denver Broncos in the first round of the 2011 NFL Draft and played with the team.

He was named the Most Valuable Player of Super Bowl 50 when the Denver Broncos defeated the Carolina Panthers.

Moreover, Miller has been selected to the Pro Bowl multiple times, showcasing his consistent excellence as an NFL player.

He has been recognized as a first-team All-Pro three times during his career, highlighting his dominance as one of the league’s top defensive players.

In his rookie season with the Denver Broncos, Miller was named the NFL Defensive Rookie of the Year.

Miller received the DeMarcus Ware Award in 2015, recognizing him as the best pass rusher in the NFL.

Miller was named to the Denver Broncos All-2010s Team, honoring his contributions during the 2010s decade.

In 2018, Miller reached 100 career sacks, a significant milestone for a pass rusher. In exchange for draft picks, he was later traded to the Los Angeles Rams during the 2021 NFL season.

After that, Von Signed a lucrative six-year, $120 million deal with the Buffalo Bills in March 2022.

Though he started the 2023 season on the reserve/PUP list, he was later activated on October 7, 2023.

Von Miller Mug Shot And Prison: Arrested And Charged For Domestic Violence

On November 30, the Dallas County Sheriff’s Office issued an arrest warrant for Buffalo Bills linebacker Von Miller in connection with a domestic violence incident.

The DeSoto Police Department has recently released NFL star Von Miller’s mugshot. He voluntarily turned himself in at the Glenn Heights Police Department at approximately 3 p.m. 

The Glenn Heights Police Department released a statement on Thursday night, confirming Miller’s surrender on the outstanding warrant.

Subsequently, he was transported to the DeSoto Regional Jail, where he was booked without incident, posted bond, and was released.

On November 29, an incident leading to Miller’s arrest happened in a Uptown Dallas condo.

The cops were called due to a verbal argument between Miller and a pregnant woman that escalated to assault.

Miller had left the scene before the police arrived, and the victim received treatment for minor injuries. The charges filed against Miller include assault of a pregnant person.

Buffalo Bills Director of Communications Kevin Kearns stated,

“This morning, we were made aware of an incident involving Von Miller. We are in the process of gathering more information and will have no further comment at this point.”

The NFL also acknowledged the matter, noting they have contacted the club but have no additional comments.

Following his arrest, Miller was released from the DeSoto Regional Jail after posting a $5,000 surety bond.

Von Miller Girlfriend: Who Is Megan Denise? 

Von Miller’s current girlfriend is not publicly known at the moment, but rumors suggest he might be involved with Megan Denise.

The duo have had an on-and-off relationship in the past. They were even allegedly expecting a baby in December 2022.

Megan Denise has turned off comments on her Instagram, sparking speculation about whether she could be the one accusing the NFL player.

Moreover, this is not the first time Von Miller has faced trouble. In 2021, Denise accused him of assault.

The Parker, Colorado Police Department investigated him following social media posts hinting at threats toward Denise, who was pregnant at the time.
