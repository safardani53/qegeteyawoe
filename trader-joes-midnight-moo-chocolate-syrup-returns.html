Trader Joe's brings back five summer must-haves and customers are running to stock up on a frozen fa

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

TRADER Joe's has announced that a slew of fan favorites are returning for summer.

The grocery store is known for its exclusive snacks that can't be found anywhere else.

Many of these Trader Joe's snacks are seasonal goods that come around once a year.

On the Inside Trader Joe's podcast, co-hosts Matt Sloan, vice president of marketing at Trader Joe's, and Tara Miller, marketing director, announced several new and returning items that are hitting shelves soon.

The U.S. Sun rounded up five familiar foods that fans can't wait to taste again.

1. TANGERINE CREAM BARS

These tangy cream bars consist of tangerine-orange sorbet and sweet, creamy vanilla ice cream in the center.

They offer a bit of nostalgia as they closely mimic the taste of an orange creamsicle.

"Don't walk, RUN," a Trader Joe's fan account wrote next to a picture of a freezer filled with the bars.

Shoppers can buy a pack of six bars for just $3.49, according to the Trader Joe's website.

2. UBE MOCHI

Another popular frozen treat is the Ube Mochi.

The treat features ube-flavored ice cream in a sweet rice-flour-based mochi wrapper.

The flavor is described as subtle but captivating.

“It's not like chocolate, it's not like vanilla. It's this really interesting thing unto itself, ube,” said Sloan.

Fans were extremely excited to hear the news, with one person tweeting: "UBE MOCHI IS BACK AT TRADER JOES" accompanied by a gif of a dancing squirrel.

3. MIDNIGHT MOO CHOCOLATE SYRUP

Fans have called this the "best chocolate syrup on the market," and they were upset when it became a seasonal item.

"Trader Joe's making their midnight moo chocolate syrup into a seasonal item which means it is gone for the foreseeable future may be my villain origin story," tweeted one disgruntled shopper.

Luckily, it's finally back on shelves after an "unstable" supply situation.

4. STRAWBERRY LEMONADE JOE-JOE'S

These cookies first launched last year and were so popular they're back for another round.

"Trader Joe's Strawberry Lemonade Joe Joes. Thank me laterrrr," tweeted a fan of the cookies.

Joe-Joe's are sandwich cookies and this flavor features two, golden, lemon-flavored wafer cookies with a strawberry creme filling featuring actual dried strawberry bits.

5. ROASTED RED PEPPER HUMMUS

Trader Joe's has offered this hummus for years, but after a dip in sales, it got a makeover.

"It is topped with a roasted red pepper relish of sorts on the top, and it smells so good," said Miller.

"It is absolutely delicious."

Despite slowed sales, the original recipe has a huge fanbase.

"Don't know what they put in it but Trader Joes red pepper hummus is the tastiest of red pepper hummuses," tweeted one shopper.

ANOTHER FAMILIAR FIND

Trader Joe's fans are celebrating another win with the return of the iconic Strawberry Lemonade Ice Bars.

The treat was specifically made for the store in Italy but will be sold in several locations across New York.

On Instagram, Trader Joe's said: "Made for us in Italy, #TraderJoes Strawberry Lemonade Ice Bars expertly imitate a summery strawberry lemonade... because, basically, they are strawberry lemonade. Only frozen. And served on a stick.

"Unlike some fruity, frozen treats, TJ’s #StrawberryLemonade Ice Bars are made without artificial flavors. Instead, they get their sweet-tart taste from strawberry purée, lemon juice concentrate, and other natural flavors. Nice."
