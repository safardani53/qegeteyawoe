What horse is Rachael Blackmore riding at Grand National 2022 today? Updated odds for Aintree

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

THE Grand National is nearly here and excitement is building more and more for the Aintree showpiece.

All eyes will be last year's champion Rachael Blackmore, who powered to victory on 11-1 Minella Times.

Blackmore, 32, followed up the triumph with success in the prestigious Gold Cup on A Plus Tard last month.

The Irish ace became the first female jockey to win the Grand National with her historic victory last year.

Blackmore said after her win: "I don't feel male or female right now. I don't even feel human."

What horse is Rachael Blackmore riding at the Grand National 2022?

It has been confirmed that Rachael Blackmore will again be riding Minella Times at this year's Grand National.
