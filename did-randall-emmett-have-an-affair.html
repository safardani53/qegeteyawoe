Did Randall Emmett have an affair?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

A NEW Hulu documentary will shed light on Randall Emmett's controversial split from Lala Kent.

Rumors have spread that there was infidelity on Emmett's part, and now fans want to know if they're true.

Did Randall Emmett have an affair?

When the couple first announced their split in October 2021, very few details were released, but weeks later, Kent appeared on Watch What Happens Live and alluded to host Andy Cohen that it had something to do with an affair.

"This has been going on for quite some time," Kent said at the time, via E! News.

"It's been kind of repeated behavior that I have found out about and it's not just one person, it's many.

"I don't know how I didn't see it," she added, noting that there were several red flags, including "the constant phone use." 

"It was glued to him," Kent continued.

"He couldn't even go into a massage without his phone on him, and he was very funny if I would get anywhere near the phone."

Emmett has not commented on Kent's allegations, but he did talk about the split during an episode of Golnesa Gharachedaghi's Genuinely GG podcast.

During the episode, Emmett said that he didn't want to "talk ill" of his ex-fiancée, but revealed that they both made "mistakes," and he insisted that "there's always two sides to every story."

"I just have to always look at the big picture...I just can't talk badly about the mother of my child," he said, via E! News.

"I just can't do it. No matter what is said about me."

While Vanderpump Rules fans might be waiting for his side, he said that he's "just not going to ever take that to the public." 

"I just don't feel it's appropriate," Emmett continued.

"I can't tell her what to do. She's a strong woman. And, you know, I just hope that in time, that kind of all mellows out."

How did Randall Emmett and Lala Kent meet?

Emmett and Kent met back in 2015 at SUR Restaurant in West Hollywood, where Kent worked.

Kent told Cosmopolitan back in 2018 that Emmett sent his assistant up to her to ask if she would be interested in being in a movie he was making.

After the process of booking the film, a connection formed.

Emmett proposed to Kent in 2018 during a dinner in Cabo, San Lucas.

A few years later, in March 2021, they welcomed a daughter, Ocean Kent Emmett, who they now co-parent.

Emmett is also the father of daughters Rylee and London, who he welcomed during his previous marriage to Ambyr Childers.

How can I watch The Randall Scandal: Love, Loathing, And Vanderpump?

The Randall Scandal: Love, Loathing, And Vanderpump is a new documentary that takes a look at the allegations surrounding the famed Hollywood producer.

Outside of the cheating allegations, Emmett has also been accused of race discrimination and workplace abuse, all of which he has continuously denied, according to Deadline.

The 90-minute documentary will feature interviews with Kent and her family as well as interviews with Felix Pire, Emmett’s high school friend, and Emmett’s former assistants, among others.

The documentary is scheduled to be released on May 22, 2023, and can be watched exclusively on Hulu.
