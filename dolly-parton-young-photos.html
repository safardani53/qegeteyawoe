Dolly Parton is 77! Celebrate the Country Queen’s birthday and career in photos here.

Now that’s what I call an icon! Dolly Parton is a singer, actress, and philanthropist who has been a country music icon for 7 decades. The songstress was born January 19, 1946 in Pittman Center, Tennessee, and started performing as a child, first singing on local radio and television programs like The Cas Walker Show on WIVK Radio and WBIR-TV in Knoxville, TN when she was just ten years old.

Dolly started recording music when she was 13 and released her debut album, Hello, Dolly, in 1967. The prolific songwriter has composed over 3000 songs, dozens of which have topped Billboard country music charts, including “Jolene” and “I Will Always Love You”. She has 11 Grammys, has appeared in hit films, and in 1999, Dolly was inducted into the Country Music Hall of Fame. In May 2022, it was revealed she would be getting inducted into the Rock And Roll Hall Of Fame.

The queen has had plenty of great moments over the years. Dolly Parton’s legendary teased tresses look great as she signs autographs in this photo from the 80s.
