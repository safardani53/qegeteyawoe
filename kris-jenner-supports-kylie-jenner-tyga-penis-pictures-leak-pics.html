Kylie showed that she’s really leaning on her mom throughout this whole ordeal by walking to the red carpet holding hands with the Kardashian momager, and embracing her in a sweet hug while posing for photos. It was hard to get a read on Kylie’s emotions, since she covered her eyes with huge sunglasses, but just to see her out and about was pretty refreshing.

Both ladies looked absolutely amazing at the premiere. Kylie showed off her incredible figure in tight, black pants and a matching crop top, with a long white jacket to complete the look. Kris, meanwhile, looked much younger than her 59 years in a black Balmain dress and strappy Brian Atwood shoes.
