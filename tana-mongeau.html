Tana Mongeau Latest News and Updates

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 21, 2024

YouTuber Tana Mongeau has not shied away from the controversy since starting her channel in 2015. 

In 2018, Tana attempted to host her own convention, aptly titled TanaCon, to compete with VidCon. However, the event did not go as planned and had to be canceled due to the venue being unable to host the number of people who showed up. Tana went on to apologize to fans and issued refunds. 

Tana went on to star on her own MTV reality series, MTV No Filter: Tana Turns 21, and has released a few singles, including "FaceTime" and "Without You." 

Her relationship with fellow YouTuber Jake Paul also made headlines, with many accusing the couple of faking their marriage for clout in July 2019. Tana removed his last name from her bio in January 2020. 

Full Name: Tana Marie Mongeau

Birthday: June 24, 1998 

Relationship Status: Single, previously married to Jake Paul, dated Bella Thorne, Brad Sousa, and Francesca Farago

Instagram: @tanamongeau

YouTube: Tana Mongeau

Net Worth: $4 million

Fun Fact: Tana won Creator of the Year at the 2019 Streamy Awards.
