Teen Mom Briana DeJesus breaks down in tears and screams hes a liar! in shocking Next Chapt

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 22, 2024

BRIANA DeJesus has broken down in tears during a shocking sneak peek for the Next Chapter reunion special.

The Teen Mom 2 star has been wrapped up in drama with her co-stars after a heated Family Reunion Season 2 preview was released this week.

Now Briana, 28, has broken down in tears in an emotional moment caught on camera during the Next Chapter reunion.

In the clip shared by MTV, fans got a glimpse into the tense season recap where the cast was joined by moderator Dr. Drew.

One troublesome part showed the reality star shaking her finger at the camera while using a tissue to stop her sobbing tears, saying: "He is a f**king liar."

BRIANA'S BRAWL

The shocking breakdown followed Briana's wild physical altercation with her co-star Ashley Jones coming to light in a preview for Teen Mom Family Reunion season two.

A teaser clip for Family Reunion season two was released on Tuesday night, as the reality stars headed on a retreat to wrap up the year.

Tensions were high as the girls' moms were invited along for an energy-filled getaway, complete with cabin parties, bungee jumping and white water rafting.

In one shocking scene from the trailer, fans watched as Ashley Jones, 25, screamed "f**k you!" to her nemesis Briana, 28, from across the room.

The comment resulted in the Florida native lunging into the air to attack her younger co-star, as security and production jumped to hold her back.

The wild moment left both cast and crew in shock as the nasty feud continues between the two Teen Moms.

STAGED SCENE?

In September, Ashley called out MTV producers for instigating the intense fight with her co-star, Briana, which took place while filming in August.

She addressed the incident during an installment of her podcast, I Need Wine.

In the episode, the 25-year-old and her mother, Tea, who was also involved in the spat, explained the events that led to the argument.

The mother-daughter duo, Briana and her mother, Roxanne, got into an altercation on set, and Ashley blamed producers for igniting the flame.

"A certain producer came to my room and asked me if I would call out a certain person and if I would disclose their business and if I would be willing to take on this great challenge," Ashley told her listeners.

Without disclosing the cast member's name, the TV star admitted that she initially declined the producers' request because she "didn't have a problem" with the person.

The ask immediately created a bad feeling for Ashley, who became suspicious of the producers' intentions.

She expressed that the conversation made her feel "disrespected" and that she was targeted out of the group to pursue the producers' "concocted plan."

The TV personality ranted: "I felt disrespected, and I felt like - I don't like when people try to play on my intelligence... you know that I've had issues with certain girls here, and you know that I'm probably the only one that's going to say or do certain things.

"So you come to me with this grand concocted plan as though I don't have a brand of my own and as though I just operate like that. I felt really f***ing disrespected at the fact that two of them came in there."

The Teen Mom star further explained that she was approached by two producers simultaneously, though one was mainly doing the talking.

"She also told me that there was going to be a confrontation between Roxy and my mom," Ashley recalled, mentioning Briana's mother and her mother, which Tea was unaware of at the time.

Producers then allegedly warned Ashley not to flee if her co-stars "attacked" her, claiming it would be harder to combine the footage.

"She also said, 'if they all attack you, just promise me you won't walk out because if you walk out, you make it really hard for editing to piece together what happened.'

"And I said, 'well, if they all attack me, I probably will walk out because who the hell wants to sit there and be attacked by multiple people?'" Ashley questioned.

Although Ashley couldn't divulge more about the dispute as it was filmed for the show, she claimed she wasn't happy with producers for "blowing up" drama for storylines.

According to The Ashley's Roundup, the fight broke out early in filming, which resulted in Ashley, Briana and their mothers being sent packing.
