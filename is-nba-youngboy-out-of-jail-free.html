Is NBA Youngboy out of jail?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 20, 2024

RAPPER NBA YoungBoy was arrested on March 22, 2021, after allegedly fleeing from officers during a traffic stop.

The 21-year-old allegedly ran away during the stop and police had to locate him with a K-9 unit.  

What jail is NBA YoungBoy in?

NBA YoungBoy was released from a Louisiana jail on October 26, 2021, to serve the remainder of his pre-trial jail time under house arrest in Utah.

Under the house arrest deal his lawyers struck, he will have to hire a private security team of former members of the military, as well as wear a GPS monitoring device.

His release will allow him to continue recording music from a home studio.

“I’m really, really happy for him,” his attorney Drew Findling told Rolling Stone after the decision.

“This is the right result. It’s the fair result.

He added: "I’m extremely overwhelmed with gratitude at the result of last week’s hearing and think this was the just decision."

The news of NBA YoungBoy's release comes after a judge ruled that in order to get out of jail he either put up $1.5million worth of property or $500,000 cash, according to TMZ.

The rapper has been locked up at the St. Martin Parish Correctional Center in Louisiana since April of 2021.

He was arrested three weeks after he allegedly fled from cops in Los Angeles.

YoungBoy was wanted on an outstanding warrant over the arrest of 16 people on drug and firearms charges in Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

At the time was given a $540,000 bond in the Central District of California. 

A California magistrate judge had granted YoungBoy pretrial release depending on Louisiana’s approval.

However, a Louisiana judge revoked his bond on April 1, 2021, citing a history of probation violations.

What was NBA YoungBoy charged with?

YoungBoy was charged with being a felon in possession of a firearm and possessing a firearm not registered to him.

The rapper pled not guilty to all charges brought against him.

A trial date is still to be determined.

YoungBoy reportedly called a fan named Raquel after receiving a letter from her while being locked up.

She recalled the encounter on Instagram, writing: “Y’all don’t know how much this shocked me I’m still in disbelief 'I fell in love with your room',

Is NBA YoungBoy in a relationship?

She is the daughter of boxing star Floyd Mayweather.

However, they split before their son was born.
