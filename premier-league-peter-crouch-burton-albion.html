Premier League legend Peter Crouch teases shock return to football as confused fans say 'am I missin

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, April 4, 2024

PETER CROUCH has teased a shock return to football with Burton Albion.

The League One club have revealed they will be making an announcement with Crouch on Monday.

The Brewers are set to welcome Cambridge to the Pirelli Stadium on Monday night.

Burton posted a snap of Crouch holding their shirt on social media, with his name and the number 15 printed on the back.

Alongside the picture, the club wrote: "A big return..."

Fans have been left shocked by the potential return of the former England striker.
