Naked artist paints unique portrait of the Queen using her BOOBS as brushes The Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 9, 2024

A BUSTY artist has painted a unique portrait of the Queen using her BOOBS as brushes.

Nadia Matievskay, from Minsk, Belarus, dips her breasts in paint and rubs them against the canvas to create her works of art.

She has captured stunning landscapes and even a new portrait of the Queen using the technique.

Nadia, who turned professional after painting her first piece as part of a challenge, angles her breasts in different directions to create unique patterns and styles.

The 21-year-old said: "I love what I do and I see why there's a demand.

"Of course I realise that the way the paintings are created is what's attracting attention to my art.

"But I want to keep on making my art as much as possible.

"It's difficult for me to say that I have a favourite painting. I love all of them."

Nadia said she had never picked up a paint brush until she began painting her unique pieces back in February.

She now sells her art across the globe on Facebook and Instagram.

Nadia says she picked up her unique style of painting while she was completing an acting course.

She said: "One day our mission was to give each other some weird tasks.

"And my task was quite odd, to paint a picture with my breast and sell it.

"So, at the start it was sort of just a joke.

"I think my first experience was quite a success.
