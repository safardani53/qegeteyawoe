Walmarts viral $8.98 blue wine flies off shelves as shoppers race to get their hands on drink - b

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

SHOPPERS have flocked to Walmart to pick up a bottle of blue wine that costs less than $10.

The Schmitt Söhne Reisling has gone viral - but not all customers seem to be huge fans of the product.

Revelers have taken to TikTok to share that they managed to get their hands on a bottle of the viral wine.

One social media influencer, known only as Monique, seemed to be a huge fan.

She poured herself several glasses as she gave her opinion on the booze.

Monique told fans that the drink tasted “good” and it was “doing what it was supposed to do.”

The Walmart shopper was so thrilled with her latest buy that she poured herself a huge glass before ranking the item a 10 out of 10.

But, another customer Necole wasn’t that impressed as she tasted the wine.

She claimed that the product had been “hyped” up too much.

And, a third shopper, known only as Daria, warned customers: “It’s not all that.”

The booze, which is 10 percent alcohol by volume, comes in a standard 750ml bottle and costs just $8.98.

It’s made using the Riesling white grape, according to the Walmart product description.

Shoppers have taken to social media apps where they’ve documented bargain buys from the retailer.

One customer, known as Jocelyn, raved over a pair of Walmart shorts, which cost only $12 but looked high-end.

Jocelyn revealed in a viral TikTok clip that the Avia Bermuda shorts are perfect for the summer season.

She paired the casual attire with a white top and converse sneakers.

Viewers admitted that they had seen the shorts on the rail at their local supercenter but were left underwhelmed.

They seemed to change their minds after watching Jocelyn’s fashion haul.

TikTokers have also raved over a long-sleeved ribbed top and the retailer’s Time and Tru cargo joggers that cost $22.
