This Morning star Dr Uchenna Okoyes cause of death revealed as family share heartbreaking tribute

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

THIS Morning star Dr Uchenna Okoye died from a bleed on the brain after collapsing at home, it has been revealed.

The medical expert, who also appeared on Channel 5 makeover show 10 Years Younger in 10 Days, passed away on Friday September 15 aged 53.

Now, it's been revealed Dr Uchenna's cause of death was a subarachnoid haemorrhage. 

A family spokesperson told MailOnline: "Dr Uchenna's sudden death was due to natural causes. We are devastated, and our hearts are broken that her beautiful, full life has been cut so short. 

"As a family, our priority right now is to take care of her young daughter and continue her legacy and keep her memory alive. A private celebration of her life will be held in the coming weeks."

It comes after her personal assistant and "bestie buddy" for 20 years Mars Webb described how Dr Okoye had a normal "busy week" before suddenly passing away.

The dental specialist had watched the musical Moulin Rouge in the West End and attended an industry event.

She had also promoted her new Mysmile dental product with clients at a lunch meeting.

Ms Webb revealed her family and friends are waiting for a coroner's report "to find out exactly what happened".

She said: "There are no suspicious circumstances surrounding her death.
