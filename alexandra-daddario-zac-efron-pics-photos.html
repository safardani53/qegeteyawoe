Pics Of Rumored Couple Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Zac Efron and Alexandra Daddario were co-stars in the 2017 movie, ‘Baywatch.’ Zac played Matt Brody, while Alexandra played Summer Quinn. The two became extremely close friends while working on the film, and fans quickly began speculating that there was something more going on between them. During promotion of the movie, the pair’s chemistry was undeniable on the redcarpet and at press events. They also left some flirty comments on one another’s Instagram photos, further fueling speculation that they were an item. While Zac and Alex never confirmed that there was anything romantic between them, it’s no secret that fans totally shipped them! After promo for the film was over, the two stayed close friends, and some people still wonder if there might be an ongoing connection between them.
