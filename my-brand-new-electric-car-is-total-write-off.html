My 22,000 car is now a total write-off after I took it on holiday

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, July 19, 2024

A DRIVER'S £22,000 car was written off after she took it on holiday.

Lisa Harding had just enjoyed a lovely stay at the Mercure Kenwood Hall Hotel, in Sheffield, but was shocked to discover a "nightmare scenario" upon leaving.

The fuming photographer, from Lincolnshire, found her pride and joy stuck after the car park flooded.

Rainwater destroyed the hybrid car leaving her stranded more than 80 miles from home.

The holidaymaker was devastated to find her plans to show relatives around the Peak District had been wrecked.

To make matters worse, Lisa claimed she received "no help" from the hotel - although a spa treatment was offered.

She vented her frustration on social media: "So there it is. My car written off due to water in the electric gear box.

"£22k car bought from brand new, my first brand new car and it’s gone over night and written off. 

"No help from hotel Mercure Sheffield and offered free dinner and a spa treatment as compensation. 

"Have to rely on a friend to drive me home tomorrow. Holiday ruined and lost my car. Absolutely devastated."
