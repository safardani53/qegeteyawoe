Susan Lucci and More Stars Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 5, 2024

The Daytime Emmys brought the best and most beloved stars of daytime TV. See all the best photos from the 2023 red carpet.

The Daytime Emmys are celebrating 50 years! The 2023 Daytime Emmys aired on December 15. Before the ceremony kicked off, the stars hit the red carpet. Celebrities from daytime talk shows and soaps looked gorgeous in a variety of looks.

During the ceremony, Susan Lucci will be honored with the Lifetime Achievement Honor. Shemar Moore will be presenting the All My Children icon with the award. Shemar notably presented Susan with her first Daytime Emmy Award in 1999 after she had been nominated in the lead actress category 19 times. She looked stunning in an orange sequin dress.

In addition to Susan, stars like Amanda Kloots, Sharon Case, and more walked the red carpet. Scroll down to see more photos of the 2023 Daytime Emmys red carpet.
