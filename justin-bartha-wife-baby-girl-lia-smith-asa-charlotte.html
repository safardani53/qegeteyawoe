Justin Bartha & Lia Smith Welcome Baby Girl

Justin must be hungover with excitement!

The Hangover actor’s wife Lia gave birth to their first child, a baby girl, on April 13, according to Us Weekly.

There is very little information about their daughter’s birth, but Lia went into labor the night before, Saturday, April 12.

On Sunday, the couple happily welcomed their daughter, Asa Charlotte, into the world.

Congrats to the happy couple on their first child!

Justin & Lia Are Still Newlyweds

Though they just welcomed their first daughter, Justin and Lia are only three months into their marriage.

The couple wed in Oahu, Hawaii in January 2014 where insiders say Lia kept her hand on her stomach throughout the entire ceremony.

According to Us, the event was star-studded with guests such as Reese Witherspoon, her husband Jim Toth, Jesse Eisenberg, and Ken Jeong.

This is the first child for both Justin and Lia, though we’re sure they’ll want to continue expanding their little family in the future!
