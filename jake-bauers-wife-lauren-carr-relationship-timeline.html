Meet Jake Bauers Wife Lauren Carr Bauers- Relationship Timeline

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

There are concerns regarding the information about the wife of Jake Bauers. The baseball player’s personal life and relationship have been on the radar attracting the attention of the fans.

Jakob Christopher Bauers is a professional American baseball player who plays for the New York Yankees in the MLB.

He is a member of the Yankees squad along with Anthony Rizzo, D.J. LeMahieu, Aaron Hicks, Harrison Bader, Willie Calhoun, and others.

Interestingly, the player bats with the right hand but throws the ball with the left one.

He previously played in MLB for the Tampa Bay Rays, Cleveland Indians, and Seattle Mariners.

Before playing in the MLB, he played amateur baseball for Ocean View Little League and high school baseball for Marina High School.

In his senior year, he led his high school team to the California Interscholastic Federation’s Southern Section Division 1 final.

Meet Jake Bauers Wife: Lauren Carr Bauers

The professional baseball player Jake Bauers married Lauren Carr, a California native.

The couple exchanged vows on January 9, 2022, at Wedgewood at Galway Downs in Temecula, California.

The adorable couple has yet to share children together.

Jake’s wife, Lauren Carr-Bauers, attended her high school at Marina High School in Huntington Beach, California.

Since they both went to the same high school, it is most likely that they first fell in love while still in high school.

She graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in communications and public relations from California State University.

Lauren completed her internship as a social media account manager at Practice Builders.

Professional Career Of Jake Bauers

On June 7, 2013, the San Diego Padres drafted Jake Bauers in the seventh round of the 2013 MLB Draft.

He spent his first professional season with the Arizona Padres of the Rookie-level Arizona League, posting a .282 batting average in 47 games.

On December 19, 2014, the Padres traded Bauers, Burch Smith, and Rene Rivera to the Tampa Bay Rays as a part of a three-team trade.

The Rays promoted him to the major leagues on June 7, 2018, and made his major league debut the same night.

On December 13, 2018, the Rays traded him to the Cleveland Indians. 

During his time with the Cleveland Indians, Jake hit 14 home runs and 49 RBIs in 160 games.

The Indians traded Bauers mid-season to the Seattle Mariners on June 10, 2021, and hit 2 home runs and 13 RBIs in 72 games for the Mariners. 

Bauers then signed a minor league contract with the Cincinnati Reds on December 21, 2022.

The Cincinnati Reds traded him to the New York Yankees on June 3, 2022, and as of this writing, he has played 5 games for the Yankees, hitting one home run.
