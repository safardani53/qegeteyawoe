Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 10, 2024

BRITNEY Spears said that she has a "new need to survive" as she posted a video of her sweaty body during an "intense workout" in Hawaii.

Britney, 40, shared a snippet of her rigorous exercise routine on Instagram. 

In the nearly two-minute video, the pop star kickstarted her workout session on her Hawaiian vacation by hula dancing while on the treadmill. 

Britney had on a tight yellow top and black shorts as her exercise outfit.

She then twirled her hair -which had purple strands - while tilting her neck back. 

The dancer took deep breaths as she pulled back on the pulley.

The Toxic singer switched between lifting weights and raising the dumbbells with her hands. 

While upside down, Britney kept her hands on the floor while straightening out her long legs.

Her back started to glisten with sweat as she reclined herself in a yoga pose and showed off her booty.

The Jessie J and Ariana Grande song, Bang Bang, played in the background. 

'MY TRIP TO MAUI'

Alongside the short vid, Britney wrote about how she was "excited for my trip to Maui."

Due to her "intense workout," the music star said she was "sweating a lot."

She captioned the post: "Honestly after being that sick before I have the need to survive in a different way. 

"Being present in an utmost way," she said as a nod to her recent conservatorship freedom.

Britney concluded: "Pushing it just a touch does wonders, in my opinion."

'VERY FEW PEOPLE KNOW'

Recently, a source close to the pop superstar exclusively revealed to The Sun that she had been secretly engaged to her fiancé  Sam Asghari, 27, since December 2020.

The engagement was reportedly kept hush-hush until her father, Jamie Spears, filed an end to her conservatorship.

The insider confessed to The Sun: “Britney told a friend that she actually got engaged to Sam during Christmas the year before last. 

“She said she had kept it on the down-low and only two people knew that they were planning on getting married that far back.  

“Britney told the friend that one of the diamonds fell out of her engagement ring and Sam let her know he was going to replace it."

The source concluded: “So when the stories about an engagement ring came out, she claimed it was really nothing new to her. 

“Britney said very few people knew because she can’t tell everybody everything.”

The Crossroads actress and her partner announced their engagement back in September 2021.
