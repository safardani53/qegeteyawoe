Kroger confirms beloved treat has been discontinued as furious shoppers demand reasons behind decisi

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 26, 2024

KROGER has confirmed it will no longer sell a fan-favorite which has left shoppers furious.

The retailer has recently discontinued a number of popular items despite customer backlash but has rarely reintroduced canceled items.

The major retailer recently revealed that it had stopped selling a hugely popular brand of BBQ chips.

Now, Kroger customers face further disappointment as it confirmed the discontinuation of its Buffalo Chicken Bites.

Twitter user SF (@StarsFA14511268) tweeted the store expressing their frustration at the discontinuation of the chicken bites.

The tweet said: "Kroger! What happened to your buffalo chicken bites?!

"Tell me why those got discontinued, while the mac and cheese bites are still a thing?"

The store replied ignoring the question but confirming the discontinuation of the buffalo bites.

Kroger replied: "Hi there! We're deeply delighted you enjoyed our Kroger Stuffed Buffalo Style Chicken Dip Bites.

"Unfortunately, these have been discontinued. We deeply apologize for any inconvenience."

The snack was made from pieces of chicken meat in a spicy cream cheese filling which was coated in a crispy breading.

Other Twitter users previously praised the buffalo chicken bites.

One wrote: "Zesty buffalo chicken bites from Kroger and Marzetti blue cheese dressing...omg, I could DIE in this so good."

Another added: "We found the best thing to make in an air fryer, they are Buffalo chicken bites from Kroger, easily the best thing I've ever had from an air fryer."

While the popular snack has been axed from the store, the Kroger YouTube channel has shown viewers how they can make a similar meal.

The channel states: "Liberating the food lovers of America, subscribe for fresh content and food inspiration."

In the video titled, "How to Make Buffalo Chicken Bites," Jennifer at Show Me the Yummy shows how viewers can make their own version.

The recipe uses Farm Rich Buffalo Style boneless chicken bites.

Viewers are instructed to thaw the packet of buffalo sauce in a bowl of hot water and chop the chicken bites into smaller pieces.

The sauce and the chicken are then mixed together until all of the bites are coated in the orange sauce.

Then, using one can of biscuit dough, you separate and flatten the dough into eight equal circles.

To each piece of dough, add a 4-ounce cube of Monterey Jack cheese.

This is followed by spooning the chicken on top in equal amounts across the dough pieces.

Jennifer then pinches the sides of the dough together at the top, enclosing the filling inside.

The balls are then placed in a baking tin with the pinched side down so they can seal during the cooking process.

Jennifer then mixes a 1/4 cup of melted butter with 1 tsp of garlic powder, and 1/4 tsp salt.

This is then drizzled on top of the dough balls and placed in the oven for 20 minutes at 375F.

The recipe recommends dipping the breaded buffalo chicken and Monterrey jack bites in ranch or blue cheese.
