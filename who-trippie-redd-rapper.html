Who is Trippie Redd and what is his net worth ?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, February 17, 2024

RAPPER, Trippie Redd, is known for his killer beats.

The 23-year-old talent is preparing to drop an album that is set to feature big-name celebrities like Travis Scott.

Who is Trippie Redd?

Michael Lamar White II - aka Trippie Redd - was born in Canton, Ohio, on June 18, 1999.

He is a rapper who got his start in the SoundCloud rap scene and has since skyrocketed to over 13.5M followers on Instagram.

Trippie Redd has released four studio albums, two of which reached the Billboard 200.

The young star has over 19million monthly listeners on Spotify and is known for his singles Dark Knight Dummo, Taking a Walk, and Topanga.

In an interview with XXL, he talked about who he grew up listening to and where his inspirations come from:

"Andre 3000. Drake. 2Pac. Tyler, The Creator. A$AP Rocky. Travis Scott. The Weeknd. Lil Wayne, T-Pain. I listen to a lot of the people that use Auto-tune," he said.

"That's why I f**k with them. At the same time, I'm versatile and I can do the lyrical thing because I'm listening to these guys for a reason. They do the same exact shit."

What is his net worth?

Trippie Redd's net worth is estimated to be $5million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

He told XXL that his career goal is to be "timeless."

"I want [my music] to be timeless, and with that goal, it's a lot of other shit that can apply to it. Grammys, billboards, all type of shit," he said.

"I'm really just trying to accomplish everything every major artist has accomplished. I want to do that and some more. I want to accomplish something for myself and set a bar for myself, for somebody to be like, 'Damn, Trippie legendary. He did such and such.'"

When is he releasing new music?

Trippie Redd has fans on the edge of their seats ever since releasing the tracklist for his upcoming album, Mansion Musik, on January 16, 2023.

The highly anticipated album is set to feature names like Chief Keef, Future, Lil Baby, Travis Scott, Kodak Black, DaBaby, and more.

According to TMZ, sources close to the project told the outlet that the rapper's album might drop as early as Friday, January 20.
