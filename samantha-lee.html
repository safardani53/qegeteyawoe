Samantha Lee Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Bio:
Samantha Lee is Tyrese Gibson‘s wife. She is originally from New Jersey and received her Masters degree in social work from University of Georgia. She currently handles cases for the Division of Family and Children Services in Georgia. She and her now-husband, actor Tyrese Gibson, were introduced by friends in 2015 and married on Valentine’s Day in 2017.

Best Known For:
Samantha Lee is best known for being Tyrese Gibson’s wife.

Personal Life:
While Tyrese never released Samantha’s name on social media, he faced backlash when he called Samantha his “black queen.” The two were first spotted together in Dec. 2016 at the 33rd Annual UNCF Mayors Masked Ball at Atlanta Marriott Marquis.
